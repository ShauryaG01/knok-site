Questions

Anything you’re wondering, answered.

Honest answers about where we search, how outreach works, your Gmail, and staying in control.

Where does Knok find roles?

Knok searches more than 150 job boards and company career pages, including LinkedIn, Naukri, Indeed, iimjobs, Instahyre, Cutshort, and specialist sources.

Does it work outside software and product roles?

Yes. You can search across technology, product, design, sales, marketing, operations, finance, consulting, legal, customer support, people, HR, administration, and other functions.

How does Knok choose who I should contact?

Knok looks for likely role owners, relevant teammates, and hiring contacts. It verifies available work emails and explains why each person may be useful to contact.

Will anything send before I approve it?

No. You review the recipient, message, resume, and follow-up schedule before a sequence starts. You can pause it at any time.

Does Knok read my Gmail inbox?

No. Knok requests permission to send approved emails from your account. It does not request permission to read or modify your inbox.

What happens when someone replies?

The remaining follow-ups stop automatically, and the reply is attached to the right conversation in your Knok activity view.

What counts as one company reachout?

One company reachout covers the approved email sequence for one company, including the first email and any follow-ups included in your plan.

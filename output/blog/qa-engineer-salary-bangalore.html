01 Salary Overview

Salary Overview

Recruiters typically write 'as per industry standards,' leave the field blank, or share a range only after the first screening call. This makes it genuinely hard to know whether you are targeting the right band before you apply, or whether an offer is fair when it lands. 02 By Experience Level

By Experience Level

QA Engineer Salary by Experience Level Experience is the biggest lever on your QA pay in Bangalore. Here is how each level breaks down

LevelExperienceCTC Range
Entry0-2 years₹4-9 LPA
Mid3-5 years₹9-17 LPA
Senior6-9 years₹17-30 LPA

Entry (₹4-9 LPA): Fresh graduates and early-career engineers land here. If you already know a scripting language, have done Selenium or API testing, or have internship-level automation work on your resume, you are more likely to hit the upper half of this band. Manual-only testers typically start at the lower end. Mid (₹9-17 LPA): This is where automation skills start moving the needle in a real way. Engineers who can own a test framework, not just write test cases but build, maintain, and scale it, sit here. Tools like Cypress, Playwright, Appium, and API testing with Postman or Rest Assured are what hiring managers look for at this level. Senior (₹17-30 LPA): Senior QA engineers are expected to define test strategy, embed quality into CI/CD pipelines (Jenkins, GitHub Actions), and mentor junior team members. Performance testing (JMeter, Gatling) and security testing exposure can push you toward the top of this band. Lead (₹28-45+ LPA): QA Leads own quality for an entire product or business unit. The salary jump here reflects not just technical depth but the responsibility for hiring, mentoring, and setting the quality bar across teams. There is natural overlap with the Senior band, the title, scope, and whether you manage people matter as much as years of experience.

04 Negotiation Tips

Negotiation Tips

How to Negotiate Your QA Salary in Bangalore 1 Anchor to the right band first. Use the ranges in this guide as your baseline. If you have 3 to 5 years of experience and strong automation skills, you belong somewhere in the ₹9-17 LPA mid-band. Walk into any negotiation with a specific number in mind, not 'it depends on the offer.' 2. Understand CTC vs. in-hand. A mid-level CTC offer can mean very different take-home depending on how much is variable pay, how much sits in allowances (HRA, food, transport), and what the employer PF contribution looks like. Always ask: 'What is the fixed, in-hand component?' before agreeing to a headline number. 3. Make your automation work concrete. Manual QA and automation QA are priced differently in Bangalore. If you have built or maintained a test framework, say so explicitly, name the tools, the coverage scale, and what you improved. A vague claim like 'I have done automation' carries far less weight than 'I built the Cypress regression suite from scratch and cut release cycle time measurably.' 4. Use a competing offer or market data as leverage. A second offer is your strongest negotiating tool. If you do not have one, reference publicly reported salary ranges and the bands in this guide. Most Bangalore recruiters will negotiate when presented with real data. 5. Do not anchor low first. If a recruiter asks for your current salary before putting a number on the table, redirect to your expected CTC range. Many candidates leave money on the table simply by revealing a low current salary before any offer is made.

05 Total Comp Breakdown

Total Comp Breakdown

Beyond Base: What Makes Up a QA CTC in Bangalore The CTC headline and the amount that hits your account each month are two different things. Here is what typically makes up a QA Engineer offer in Bangalore

ComponentWhat to Know
Fixed base salaryYour guaranteed monthly pay, the number that matters most for day-to-day finances
Variable / performance payPaid quarterly or annually on hitting targets; the fixed-to-variable split varies widely by company, so always ask
ESOPs / RSUsCommon at product companies; vesting of 4 years with a 1-year cliff is commonly cited in the market
Provident Fund (PF)Statutory employer contribution included in CTC, it is not take-home
Health insuranceGroup medical cover for self and sometimes family; publicly reported covers vary widely by company size
AllowancesHRA, food, transport, these affect your effective tax rate and net monthly salary

At IT services firms, variable pay is often tied to company-wide performance rather than your individual output, making it less predictable than it looks on paper. At funded product startups, ESOPs can represent significant upside if the company has a liquidity event, but they are illiquid until then. Treat stock as a bonus, not a guarantee, when comparing two offers side by side.

Q Questions

Frequently asked

What is the average QA Engineer salary in Bangalore in 2026?

Based on knok jobradar data covering 80 active QA Engineer roles in Bangalore (as of 8 July 2026), salaries range from ₹4 LPA at entry level to ₹45+ LPA for Lead roles. The mid-level band (3-5 years experience) sits at ₹9-17 LPA, which is where the largest share of active openings are typically concentrated. Quoting a single 'average' is misleading because the range is wide, your automation skills, company type, and specific domain matter more than any single average figure.

Is there a big pay difference between manual and automation QA in Bangalore?

Yes, automation QA consistently commands higher pay than manual QA at every experience level, according to industry surveys and Glassdoor data. Bangalore companies, especially product firms, are largely hiring for engineers who can build and own test frameworks, not just write test cases. If you are currently in manual QA, moving to automation starting with Selenium, Cypress, or Playwright is the single highest-return skill investment you can make for your salary trajectory.

Do QA Engineers in Bangalore typically get ESOPs?

At product startups and many mid-size tech companies in Bangalore, ESOPs are commonly included in QA packages from the mid-level upward. Vesting schedules of 4 years with a 1-year cliff are commonly cited across the Bangalore market. That said, ESOPs only pay off if the company is acquired or goes public, always read the ESOP agreement and treat stock as potential upside rather than part of your core compensation when comparing offers.

How does Bangalore compare to other Indian cities for QA opportunities?

Bangalore had 80 active QA Engineer openings tracked by knok jobradar on 8 July 2026, more than Delhi NCR (58), Chennai (13), Pune (12), Hyderabad (6), and Mumbai (4). Beyond volume, Bangalore's density of product and SaaS companies means QA roles there tend to involve deeper automation, CI/CD integration, and framework ownership compared to the services-heavy hiring in other cities. Glassdoor and industry surveys also commonly rank Bangalore among the top-paying cities for QA professionals in India.

What skills push a QA Engineer toward the higher salary bands in Bangalore?

Automation frameworks, Selenium, Cypress, Playwright, Appium, paired with API testing (Rest Assured, Postman) and CI/CD integration (Jenkins, GitHub Actions) are what most Bangalore hiring managers look for at mid and senior levels. Performance testing tools like JMeter or Gatling, and any exposure to security or SAST testing, are more niche but commonly cited as justification for premium pay at the senior band. Domain expertise in fintech, SaaS, or healthtech also strengthens your negotiating position, especially for lead roles.

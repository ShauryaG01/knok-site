01 Salary Overview

Salary Overview

Software Engineer Salaries in Bangalore: What the Data Actually Shows Here is the frustrating reality most job seekers discover mid-process: the majority of Software Engineer listings in India do not show salary at all. Of the 958 active Software Engineer openings in Bangalore tracked by knok jobradar as of July 2026, pay ranges are rarely disclosed upfront, so candidates go into interviews without a clear anchor. The bands below are drawn from knok jobradar data and are broadly consistent with what Glassdoor and industry surveys commonly cite for Bangalore. The city still commands a premium over most other Indian tech hubs, driven by the concentration of product companies, MNCs, and funded startups. At a glance:
- Freshers (0-2 years): 6-12 LPA
- Mid-level (3-5 years): 15-25 LPA
- Senior (6-9 years): 28-45 LPA
- Lead / Staff (10+ years): 40-65+ LPA These are base salary figures. Total compensation, once you factor in variable pay and ESOPs, can look quite different, especially at well-funded product companies. The sections below break this down by experience, city, and comp structure.

04 Negotiation Tips

Negotiation Tips

How to Negotiate Your Software Engineer Salary 1 Do not reveal your current salary first
The salary-history question is increasingly being challenged by candidates across India. Redirect it: *"I am targeting market rate for this role and my experience level, can you share the band?"* Most recruiters will engage. 2. Ask for the band early
If a company will not share the salary band before an interview loop, that is useful information too. When you do negotiate, anchor within the range you are targeting, you can only negotiate down from there, not up. 3. Price the full package, not just base
A mid-level base with strong ESOPs at a funded startup can outperform a higher base at a stable MNC over a three-to-five year horizon, depending on the company's trajectory. Get the numbers for variable pay, joining bonus, and ESOPs on paper before comparing. 4. Competing offers are your strongest lever
If you have another offer, even one you would not take, mentioning it changes the conversation. Recruiters are substantially more flexible when there is genuine competition. Running parallel processes is worth the effort. 5. Time your ask correctly
Negotiate after the verbal offer, not during the interview rounds. Once they have decided they want you, you have far more leverage than at any earlier stage. 6. Ask about joining bonus and notice buyout
Many Bangalore companies offer joining bonuses to offset notice period costs. If your current employer will not relieve you early, ask the new company to cover the buyout, it is a standard ask and often easier to get approved than a base salary increase.

Senior engineers on levels.fyi commonly report treating ESOP value as upside rather than guaranteed income, a useful frame when you are comparing offers.

Q Questions

Frequently asked

What is the Software Engineer salary range in Bangalore in 2026?

Based on knok jobradar data as of July 2026, freshers earn 6-12 LPA, mid-level engineers 15-25 LPA, seniors 28-45 LPA, and leads or staff engineers 40-65+ LPA. These figures reflect base salary; total compensation including bonuses and ESOPs can be higher at product companies and funded startups. Most listings in Bangalore do not disclose pay upfront, so these bands are best used as a reference point going into negotiations.

Is Bangalore still the highest-paying city for Software Engineers in India?

Bangalore leads on both volume, 958 active Software Engineer openings as of July 2026, and salary. It is commonly cited alongside Hyderabad as India's top-paying tech market. Hyderabad has closed the gap meaningfully over the past few years and sits at 183 active openings. Delhi/NCR and Pune are competitive for specific company types, but Bangalore still has the highest concentration of high-paying product engineering roles.

How much of a salary hike should I target when switching jobs?

There is no single right answer, but publicly reported data on Glassdoor and levels.fyi consistently shows that job switchers receive larger increments than those who rely on internal appraisals. A competing offer is the most effective negotiation lever, it anchors expectations and gives you real leverage. Focus on negotiating the full package (base, bonus, joining bonus, ESOPs) rather than just the base number, since some companies have more flexibility in one bucket than another.

Do service companies pay as much as product companies for Software Engineers?

No, the gap is significant and widely acknowledged. Publicly reported data on levels.fyi and Glassdoor consistently shows product companies and MNC engineering centres paying more, especially at mid and senior levels. The 15-25 LPA mid-level band reflects product-facing roles; service company equivalents are commonly cited as lower. If maximising salary is your primary goal, targeting product companies and growth-stage startups in Bangalore is the standard approach.

How should I think about ESOPs when comparing job offers?

ESOPs at Indian startups and RSUs at public companies or large MNCs can add real value, but they come with conditions worth reading carefully. Vesting schedules commonly follow a four-year period with a one-year cliff, meaning you receive nothing if you leave before the cliff. At early-stage startups, ESOPs may be worth very little if the company does not reach a liquidity event. Senior engineers on levels.fyi commonly recommend treating ESOPs as upside rather than guaranteed income, and factoring the cliff date into any decision to join or leave.

01 Salary Overview

Salary Overview

Most Data Scientist job listings in India do not display a salary range. You find out the number only after spending time applying, clearing rounds, and waiting for an offer. The figures here come from knok jobradar, which tracked 46 active Data Scientist openings in Delhi NCR as of 8 July 2026.

Across those roles, pay ranges from 8 LPA at entry level to 70+ LPA for Lead and Principal positions. That spread is wide because the title 'Data Scientist' gets used loosely: it covers a fresher writing basic Python scripts and a senior professional building production ML systems at scale. Your experience, the tools you know, and the type of company (startup, IT services firm, or large MNC) all shift where you land in that range.

The sections below break the numbers down by experience level and by city so you can find the band that is realistic for your profile right now.

Entry (8-16 LPA): At this stage, employers want to see that you can work with data end to end. Strong Python, a solid grasp of ML fundamentals, and at least one real project, whether an internship, a Kaggle competition, or a personal build, matter more than your college tier.

Mid (18-30 LPA): The jump from entry to mid is the most significant in the table. Once you can own a data problem independently from cleaning and feature engineering to model deployment and monitoring, your market value rises fast. Domain expertise in fintech, healthcare, or e-commerce pushes you toward the upper half of this band.

Senior (30-48 LPA): Senior Data Scientists are expected to make architectural decisions, mentor junior team members, and work closely with product and business stakeholders. Expertise in MLOps, model governance, or a niche like NLP or computer vision commands the top of this range.

Lead and Principal (45-70+ LPA): These roles shift from hands-on modelling to strategy, hiring, and cross-functional influence. The 70+ LPA figure applies to Principal Scientists at large product companies or senior positions at high-growth startups with strong ESOP packages.

04 Negotiation Tips

Negotiation Tips

Know your band before any call. Walk into every salary conversation knowing the range for your experience level. For a mid-level Data Scientist in Delhi NCR, that is 18-30 LPA. Anchor your ask toward the upper half if your portfolio is strong or you bring domain expertise the team clearly needs.

Separate fixed pay from total CTC. Many companies structure CTC to include variable pay, retiral contributions, and allowances. Ask specifically: 'What is the fixed monthly salary, and what portion is variable or performance-linked?' Variable pay is never guaranteed and should not be treated the same as base.

Use a competing offer as leverage. A real, written competing offer is the strongest negotiating tool you have. If you have one, mention it professionally and early. Most companies will at least match it to close you.

Negotiate beyond the salary number. If a company is stuck on its salary budget, ask about a joining bonus to offset notice period loss, extra leaves, a faster first appraisal cycle, or remote work flexibility. These have real monetary and lifestyle value.

Time it right. Do not negotiate during the first HR screening call. Wait until after you receive the written offer letter. That is when your leverage is highest: they have chosen you, and switching to another candidate has a cost.

Get everything in writing. Verbal commitments about bonuses, appraisals, or role title upgrades are not enforceable. Confirm that anything promised is included in the offer letter before you sign.

05 Total Comp Breakdown

Total Comp Breakdown

A Data Scientist's CTC in India is rarely just one number. It is a bundle of components, and understanding each one is essential to comparing two offers accurately.

Fixed base salary is the predictable part: credited to your account every month before tax and PF deductions. This is the figure to anchor any negotiation on, and the one that determines your PF contribution.

Variable pay is a performance-linked component paid quarterly or annually, based on individual and company targets. The size varies widely by employer and the payout is never guaranteed. Always ask what portion of your CTC is variable before you accept an offer.

Stock options (ESOPs) appear mainly at startups and product-first companies. They vest over multiple years with a cliff period, meaning you lose them if you leave too early. ESOPs can add significant paper value but are illiquid until a buyback event, secondary sale, or IPO.

Retiral benefits include your Provident Fund contributions, which both you and your employer make each month from your basic salary. This is real money but locked until you meet the withdrawal criteria or complete the required service period.

Allowances cover HRA, Leave Travel Allowance, medical reimbursement, and internet or phone allowance in many companies. When structured correctly, these reduce your taxable income meaningfully. Always ask HR for an in-hand take-home estimate based on your tax slab, not just the CTC headline.

Q Questions

Frequently asked

What is a realistic starting salary for a fresher Data Scientist in Delhi NCR in 2026?

Based on knok jobradar data, the entry-level band for Data Scientists in Delhi NCR is 8-16 LPA. Where you land within that band depends on your college, internship experience, and whether you can show a real end-to-end project. Freshers with strong Kaggle rankings, published work, or a reputed internship tend to get offers closer to the top of this range.

How does Delhi NCR compare to Bangalore for Data Science job opportunities?

Bangalore had 166 open Data Scientist roles in the knok jobradar snapshot, compared to 46 in Delhi NCR. Bangalore's advantage comes from its higher density of product companies and funded startups, which drive consistent hiring. Delhi NCR has demand too, particularly from IT services firms, consulting companies, and government-adjacent tech organisations, but the total number of options is smaller.

What skills push a Data Scientist's salary toward the top of the range?

At entry level, Python and ML fundamentals matter most. From mid level upward, MLOps experience, the ability to deploy and monitor models in production, and deep domain expertise in a high-value sector like fintech or healthcare are the biggest salary drivers. Niche skills in NLP, computer vision, or large language model fine-tuning are increasingly in demand and publicly reported on Glassdoor and levels.fyi to command meaningful premiums.

Should I relocate from Delhi NCR to Bangalore for a better Data Science salary?

The job market in Bangalore is much larger, with 166 openings versus 46 in Delhi NCR based on the same snapshot. Publicly reported data on Glassdoor suggests senior roles in Bangalore can offer higher pay, partly because of the concentration of product companies. However, the higher cost of living in Bangalore, particularly rent, can offset part of that gain, so the right answer depends on your level, target company type, and personal priorities.

When is the right time to ask for a salary raise after joining?

Most companies in India run annual appraisal cycles, so the formal opportunity comes once a year. If your market value has grown faster than your appraisal increment, the most effective move is to get a competing offer and use it to negotiate internally. It is worth asking during your initial offer negotiation for a faster first appraisal cycle, such as at the six-month mark, especially if you are joining below your target range.

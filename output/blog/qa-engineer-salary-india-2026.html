01 Salary Overview

Salary Overview

Most QA Engineer job listings in India do not display salary. Of the 414 open roles tracked by knok jobradar as of 2026-07-08, the large majority carry no pay figure at all, so candidates are often guessing whether a role is even worth applying to. The ranges below are drawn from listings that do disclose pay, alongside publicly reported industry surveys; treat them as honest benchmarks, not guarantees.

Automation skills, Selenium, Cypress, Playwright, API testing, commonly command a visible pay premium over manual testing at every band. If your work is still primarily manual, moving to automation is the fastest lever on your salary.

02 By Experience Level

By Experience Level

Entry Level (0-2 years), 4 to 9 LPA
Freshers and early-career QAs usually start in manual testing, writing test cases, running regression cycles, and logging defects. Pay sits between 4 LPA and 9 LPA depending on company type. A product company or funded startup typically offers more than an IT services firm for the same profile. Even basic automation exposure during an internship or college project shifts your offer upward. Mid Level (3-5 years), 9 to 17 LPA
This is where automation experience begins to show clearly in offer letters. If you can build and maintain automated test suites, understand CI/CD pipelines, and own a test strategy for a feature team, you are solidly in this band. The upper end, closer to 17 LPA, is typically reserved for product companies and MNCs where quality engineering is treated as a first-class discipline. Senior Level (6-9 years), 17 to 30 LPA
Senior QEs are expected to set quality direction for a team, not just execute tests. Performance testing, security testing basics, and the ability to shift quality left in the engineering process separate the 17 LPA offers from the 30 LPA ones. Domain expertise in fintech, healthtech, or e-commerce adds further weight to your profile. Lead / Manager, 28 to 45+ LPA
QA Leads and Engineering Managers overseeing quality functions reach 28-45+ LPA at larger product companies. SDET-track leads at MNCs or high-growth startups can push further, especially if they bring cross-functional influence, working across product, infra, and security teams, alongside deep technical depth.

Listings won't tell you what a role pays, but knok will tell you which ones you're competitive for.

04 Negotiation Tips

Negotiation Tips

1. Ask for the CTC breakup in writing
Always request a detailed offer breakup before signing. Variable pay, joining bonus, and retiral contributions can inflate the headline CTC significantly. What matters most is the fixed in-hand component, ask for that number separately before you compare offers. 2. Use competing offers directly
A competing offer is the most reliable lever in salary negotiation in India. State it plainly: 'I have an offer at X LPA from another company, I would prefer to join here. Can you match or come close?' You do not need to be aggressive; direct and calm works well. 3. Benchmark before you pick up the phone
Platforms like Glassdoor and levels.fyi carry publicly reported salary data for QA roles across companies and cities. Cross-check them before your negotiation call so you know what the market actually pays, not just what the recruiter tells you. 4. Automation skills justify a higher ask
If you have hands-on automation experience, say so explicitly in the negotiation. Automation QEs are commonly cited by hiring managers as harder to find than manual testers, and companies price that scarcity into their offers. Make sure you are pricing it in too. 5. Ask about a joining bonus when base is stuck
If the company cannot move on fixed pay, ask about a one-time joining bonus. Many firms, especially product companies, have more flexibility on one-time payments than on recurring salary bands tied to internal structures. 6. Negotiate notice buyout separately
If your current employer will not relieve you early, ask your new company to cover the buyout cost. This is a standard ask in Indian job negotiations and many companies, particularly in the product and startup space, agree to it without pushing back hard.

05 Total Comp Breakdown

Total Comp Breakdown

When an employer quotes you a CTC number, it is rarely just your take-home pay. Here is what that number typically contains:

ComponentWhat it is
Basic SalaryCore fixed pay; forms the base for PF and gratuity calculations
HRAHouse Rent Allowance, partially tax-exempt if you pay rent
Special / Flexi AllowanceA filler component; varies widely by company
Variable PayPerformance-linked; the share of CTC it represents is commonly cited as varying widely across companies and levels
PF (Employer Contribution)Statutory employer share of Provident Fund, included in CTC by most companies
GratuityStatutory benefit payable after you complete the minimum service period; included in CTC even though you receive it only on exit
Medical / InsuranceGroup health insurance; cover value varies by company
ESOPs / RSUsCommon at startups and listed product companies; can be significant at senior levels but vest over time

What this means for you: Always ask for the fixed in-hand monthly figure before comparing offers. Two CTCs that look identical on paper can mean very different take-home amounts depending on how variable pay and retiral components are structured. For ESOPs and RSUs: publicly reported vesting schedules commonly follow a multi-year cliff-and-gradual-release pattern. Do not treat unvested equity as income you can count on today.

Q Questions

Frequently asked

What is the starting salary for a QA Engineer fresher in India in 2026?

Entry-level QA Engineers with 0-2 years of experience typically earn between 4 LPA and 9 LPA. Where you land in that range depends mostly on company type, product firms and funded startups tend to pay more than IT services companies for the same profile. Freshers with automation exposure or a strong internship track record usually receive offers closer to the upper end of the band.

Is QA a well-paying career in India compared to software development?

At entry and mid levels, QA salaries in India do tend to run lower than software development salaries, this is publicly reported across platforms like Glassdoor and levels.fyi. The gap narrows considerably at the senior and lead level, especially for SDET and performance engineering roles. Companies that treat quality as a core engineering function pay QA leads very competitively.

Which city has the most QA Engineer job openings right now?

Based on knok jobradar data as of 2026-07-08, Bangalore leads with 80 open QA roles, followed by Delhi NCR at 58. Chennai has 13 openings and Pune has 12, with Hyderabad at 6 and Mumbai at 4. This snapshot covers 414 listings in total, a broad but not exhaustive view of the market. The number of remote-friendly QA roles has also grown, so your city of residence matters less than it once did.

How much does a senior QA Engineer earn in India?

Senior QA Engineers with 6-9 years of experience typically earn between 17 LPA and 30 LPA. The higher end of this range is usually reached at product companies, MNCs, or well-funded startups where quality engineering is valued at par with development. Specialisations like performance testing, security QA, or mobile automation are commonly cited as pushing offers toward the upper end of that band.

What skills help a QA Engineer earn more in India?

Automation is the single biggest differentiator, engineers who can build and own test frameworks using tools like Selenium, Playwright, Cypress, or RestAssured earn more than manual testers at every experience level. Beyond automation, skills in performance testing, API testing, CI/CD integration, and cloud platforms are commonly cited in industry surveys as premium QA skill sets in 2026. Domain expertise in fintech or healthtech adds further weight.

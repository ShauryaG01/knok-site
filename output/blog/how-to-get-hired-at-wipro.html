01 Hiring Overview

Hiring Overview

Wipro is one of India's largest IT services and consulting companies, with global operations and a wide range of roles in software engineering, testing, data analytics, cloud, cybersecurity, and business process services. As of July 2026, Wipro has 182 open roles listed across its hiring channels, covering both freshers and experienced professionals.

Wipro hires through two main tracks. The fresher track targets engineering graduates and MCA students through campus drives and off-campus tests. The experienced track is for professionals with two or more years of work in IT, consulting, or BPO. Both tracks lead to a structured interview process, though the stages differ slightly by level.

Wipro posts roles on its own careers portal and on major Indian job boards. Positions span cities including Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Pune, Chennai, Kolkata, and Mumbai, with remote and hybrid options available depending on the business unit and project.

03 Interview Process

Interview Process

The Wipro hiring process typically has four to five stages, though the exact number can vary by role, seniority, and business unit.

Stage 1: Online Application. Submit your resume through Wipro's careers portal or a partner job board. For fresher drives, you may receive a link to register for an off-campus test. For experienced roles, a recruiter usually screens your profile first.

Stage 2: Aptitude and Technical Online Test. Freshers typically sit a timed online test covering quantitative aptitude, logical reasoning, verbal English, and coding (commonly two coding problems). Experienced candidates may skip this stage or take a shorter technical assessment instead.

Stage 3: Technical Interview (one or two rounds). A panel of Wipro engineers asks questions relevant to your domain. For software roles, expect questions on data structures, algorithms, object-oriented programming, and a language you have listed on your resume. For domain-specific roles such as SAP, testing, or cloud, expect questions on those tools and any certifications you hold. This stage may happen online or in person.

Stage 4: Managerial Round (for senior roles). Roles above three or four years of experience sometimes include a managerial round where the interviewer checks project experience, leadership, and problem-solving approach.

Stage 5: HR Interview. An HR partner discusses offer expectations, relocation, notice period, and confirms background details. This round also tests communication and cultural fit.

Offers typically arrive within two to four weeks of clearing the HR round, though timelines can stretch during high-volume hiring cycles.

04 What They Look For

What They Look For

Wipro looks for a combination of technical depth, clear communication, and a team-first attitude. Here is a breakdown of what typically matters at each level:

LevelTechnical focusSoft skills focus
Fresher (0-1 yr)DSA, core CS fundamentals, one coding languageLearning mindset, clarity in explaining answers
Junior (2-4 yrs)Domain tools, debugging, system design basicsOwnership, meeting deadlines
Mid-senior (5+ yrs)Architecture, client communication, certificationsLeadership, stakeholder management

Strong written and spoken English matters across all levels because Wipro teams often work with international clients. Candidates who explain their thinking clearly, even when they do not know an exact answer, tend to do better than those who go silent under pressure.

Certifications in cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP), SAP modules, Salesforce, or cybersecurity tools are a genuine advantage and sometimes a stated requirement for specific roles.

05 How To Prepare

How To Prepare

For freshers: Practise aptitude questions covering time, speed, distance, percentages, and syllogisms on free online platforms. Also work through a solid set of coding problems covering arrays, strings, linked lists, and sorting. Wipro's online test has a coding section, so do not skip this even if you are applying for a non-core role.

For experienced candidates: Refresh your knowledge of the tools and technologies on your resume, because interviewers tend to go deep on whatever you have listed. Prepare two or three solid project stories using the situation-task-action-result format. Have honest, specific answers ready for questions like 'what was the biggest challenge in your last project' and 'how did you handle a conflict with a teammate.'

General tips:
- Read Wipro's public sustainability and strategy reports from 2024 or 2025 so you can speak to the company's direction in the HR round.
- Be clear about your notice period and salary expectations before the HR round, not during it.
- For remote interviews, test your camera, microphone, and internet connection the night before.
- Follow up politely with the recruiter if you have not heard back within two weeks of your final round.

06 Culture And Values

Culture And Values

Wipro describes its culture through what it calls the 'Spirit of Wipro,' which centres on being intensely customer-focused, treating each person with respect, and acting with sensitivity toward communities and the environment. In practice, interviewers pay close attention to how you speak about past employers and colleagues.

Wipro places a strong emphasis on continuous learning. The company runs an internal learning platform and encourages employees to earn certifications. Mentioning a recent course or certification during your interview signals that you fit this culture well.

The company has made public commitments to diversity and inclusion, with programmes targeting women returning to the workforce and candidates from varied educational backgrounds.

On work style: project teams at Wipro are often large and matrix-structured, meaning you may report to both a delivery manager and a practice lead. Comfort with structured processes, documentation, and cross-team coordination goes a long way.

Q Questions

Frequently asked

Does Wipro hire freshers directly from college?

Yes. Wipro runs on-campus placement drives at engineering colleges and also holds off-campus drives open to all graduates. The fresher process usually starts with an online test, followed by a technical interview and an HR round. Registrations for off-campus batches are announced on Wipro's careers page and on LinkedIn.

How long does the Wipro hiring process take?

For freshers, the process from online test to offer letter can take four to eight weeks, especially during mass hiring seasons. For experienced candidates, timelines can be shorter for urgent roles but may stretch to six weeks if multiple interview rounds are needed. Following up with your recruiter every ten days is perfectly normal.

What salary can I expect at Wipro?

Wipro does not publish a fixed salary grid publicly. Glassdoor and AmbitionBox list commonly cited compensation ranges for various roles and experience levels, so those are the best places to check current figures. Your final offer depends on your experience, skills, the specific business unit, and how well you negotiate in the HR round.

Is there a bond or service agreement for freshers?

Wipro has at times required freshers joining through certain programmes to serve a minimum period, typically cited as one year in industry discussions. The terms can change from batch to batch. Read your offer letter carefully before signing and ask the HR partner to clarify any clauses you do not fully understand.

Can I apply for multiple roles at Wipro at the same time?

Generally you can apply to more than one open position, but Wipro's internal policy sometimes limits simultaneous active applications. The safest approach is to let your recruiter know which roles interest you most and allow them to guide the process. Applying through a single point of contact, either a recruiter or an employee referral, often avoids confusion.

Does an employee referral improve my chances?

Yes, a referral from a current Wipro employee typically moves your resume to the top of the recruiter's queue and signals a basic vetting from inside the company. Referrals do not guarantee an offer, but they meaningfully increase the chance that your application gets reviewed quickly. Reaching out to Wipro employees on LinkedIn with a polite, specific message is a practical way to request one.

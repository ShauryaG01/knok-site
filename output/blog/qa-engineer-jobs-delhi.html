05 How To Stand Out

How To Stand Out

QA in 2026 is not just about finding bugs manually. Hiring managers in Delhi NCR look for a few things that consistently separate candidates who get called back from those who do not.

Automation skills are the baseline, not a bonus. Selenium WebDriver, Cypress, and Playwright are the most commonly requested tools in active Delhi NCR job descriptions. If you can build and maintain a test framework rather than just run existing scripts, make that explicit in your resume with a GitHub link or a specific project mention.

API testing knowledge is expected at mid level. Tools like Postman and RestAssured appear in a large share of mid-level and senior job descriptions. If you have not worked with API testing yet, Postman is a straightforward skill to pick up and adds visible weight to your profile.

Performance and security testing open senior doors. JMeter, k6, and basic OWASP awareness show up in senior listings, especially at product companies. These skills are less common in the candidate pool, so even a personal project (testing a public API under load, for example) gives you a concrete talking point in interviews.

ISTQB certification helps at entry level, less so after. It signals baseline knowledge to recruiters screening hundreds of resumes and is worth getting early in your career. At mid and senior levels, a strong portfolio of test frameworks on GitHub matters more than any certification.

Research the company before you apply. Deutsche Telekom Digital Labs handles telecom-grade software where reliability and regression coverage are critical. GreyOrange is all about robotics and logistics automation. Tailoring even one line of your application to the company's domain shows genuine interest and takes very little effort.

06 Application Strategy

Application Strategy

Start by mapping which companies match your experience level. Deutsche Telekom Digital Labs (24 roles) and GreyOrange (10 roles) are the highest-volume targets, together accounting for just over half the tracked Delhi NCR openings. For smaller companies like Zippee, VegaPay, and Shipsy, a direct LinkedIn message to the hiring manager or engineering lead often outperforms applying only through the company careers page.

Tailor your resume to automation keywords. Most Delhi NCR companies use ATS systems that filter resumes before a human sees them. Name your tools explicitly: Selenium, Cypress, JMeter, Postman, RestAssured. Do not bury them only inside project descriptions.

Apply early in the posting cycle. QA roles at funded startups typically close fast once a strong candidate appears. Applying within the first week of a posting going live improves your callback rate meaningfully.

Follow up after you apply. A short, polite LinkedIn message to the recruiter or QA lead, sent three to four days after applying, is standard practice in Delhi NCR hiring and rarely hurts. Keep it brief: mention the role, name one relevant skill, and say you would welcome a conversation.

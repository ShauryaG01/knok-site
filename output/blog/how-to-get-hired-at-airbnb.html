01 Hiring Overview

Hiring Overview

Airbnb is one of the most recognisable global tech brands, and right now it has 330 open roles across engineering, product, design, data, operations, and corporate functions. That is a meaningful number of openings, and a real window for well-prepared candidates to get in. The hiring process at Airbnb is structured and deliberate. Expect multiple rounds, interviewers who use clear rubrics, and a genuine emphasis on cultural alignment alongside domain skill. This is not a company where a sharp resume alone carries you to an offer, the values interview carries real weight at every level. Airbnb hires globally and has a strong remote-friendly stance for many roles. Indian candidates targeting global positions will typically go through the same process as any other applicant: resume screen, recruiter call, technical or domain interviews, and a final panel loop.

03 Interview Process

Interview Process

Stage 1, Application and Resume Screen
Airbnb's recruiting team reviews applications for keyword and experience alignment with the specific role. Generic CVs rarely clear this stage. Tailor your resume to the job description every time. Stage 2, Recruiter Call
If shortlisted, a recruiter reaches out for a brief screening call. Expect questions about your background, notice period, salary expectations, and why Airbnb. Keep answers crisp and honest. This call also sets expectations for the rounds ahead. Stage 3, Hiring Manager or Technical Screen
For tech roles, this is typically a live coding or system-design discussion. For non-tech roles, product, marketing, operations, expect a case study or a deep-dive into past experience. This stage helps the team decide whether to bring you into the full loop. Stage 4, Virtual Onsite / Interview Loop
This is the core of the process. Multiple back-to-back rounds covering:
- Technical or domain depth (role-specific)
- Cross-functional collaboration scenarios
- Core Values interview, Airbnb treats this as a hard filter, not a formality
- Occasionally a take-home project or short presentation Stage 5, Offer and Negotiation
If all rounds go well, the recruiter calls with a verbal offer followed by a written one. Levels.fyi and Glassdoor carry publicly reported Airbnb compensation data useful for benchmarking at this stage. The full process from application to offer commonly takes several weeks, based on publicly reported candidate experiences. Once the loop begins, rounds are typically completed within a couple of weeks.

04 What They Look For

What They Look For

Airbnb looks for people who are strong in their domain *and* genuinely aligned with what the company stands for. Interviewers are trained to probe both, and one without the other rarely results in an offer. Technical or Domain Excellence
For engineers, solid fundamentals, data structures, algorithms, system design, are non-negotiable. For product managers, structured thinking and a track record of shipped products. For operations or business roles, analytical rigour and stakeholder management are key. Belong Anywhere Mindset
Airbnb's 'Belong Anywhere' mission shapes how interviews are run. Interviewers ask specifically about times you created inclusive environments, worked across cultures, or showed empathy under pressure. Vague or rehearsed answers are easy to spot. Ownership and Initiative
Airbnb values people who identify a problem and drive it to resolution without waiting to be told. Come with specific stories where you led something end-to-end, even if it was not in your job description. Communication Clarity
Every role at Airbnb requires working across functions. Being able to explain your thinking clearly, especially in writing, is heavily weighted. This shows up in how interviewers evaluate your answers, not just in take-home tasks.

05 How To Prepare

How To Prepare

Tune Your Resume First
Match the language in the job description. Airbnb's applicant tracking system filters on keywords before a human reads your CV. Spend time on this before hitting apply. Prepare Several Detailed Stories for the Values Round
The Core Values interview is where many candidates lose offers at Airbnb. Have specific, real examples ready for: a time you championed inclusion or belonging, a time you took ownership beyond your role, a time you disagreed with a decision and how you handled it, and a time you made a difficult tradeoff under pressure. Generic answers do not land. For Engineers: Breadth First, Then Depth
Airbnb coding rounds test standard DSA. Practise at medium-to-hard level. For senior roles, system design, distributed systems, APIs, scale, is essential. Airbnb's publicly available engineering blog is a useful preparation resource for understanding the kind of problems their teams solve. Research the Product Hands-On
Use Airbnb as a guest or host if you can. Interviewers notice the difference between candidates with genuine product knowledge versus those giving surface-level answers. Benchmark Salary Before the Recruiter Call
Levels.fyi and Glassdoor carry publicly reported Airbnb compensation data. Know your range before the call so the salary conversation does not catch you off guard. 06 Culture And Values

Culture And Values

Airbnb's culture is built around a set of core values that are used actively in hiring decisions, not just displayed on office walls. Understanding these before your interviews gives you a real edge. Champion the Mission
Every employee is expected to genuinely care about helping people 'Belong Anywhere.' This shows up in product decisions, team feedback, and how people run meetings, not just in HR conversations. Be a Host
Airbnb uses the host metaphor internally: anticipate needs, take responsibility for the experience, and make people feel welcome. Candidates who naturally think this way stand out in collaboration and behavioural rounds. Embrace the Adventure
Curiosity, risk-taking, and learning from failure are valued. Airbnb has navigated significant pivots, especially during 2024-2025, and the culture rewards people who adapt without losing focus. Be a Cereal Entrepreneur
The 'cereal' reference comes from a famous chapter in Airbnb's founding story. It signals scrappiness, doing unconventional things to make progress when resources are tight. Simplify
Clear communication and simple solutions over complicated ones are valued across all functions. If you can cut through ambiguity and explain your reasoning without jargon, that is noticed. Every Frame Matters
Airbnb is a design-led company. Quality and craft, in product, communication, and work output, are held to a high standard company-wide, not just in design roles. Sloppy written communication in interviews is a red flag.

Q Questions

Frequently asked

Does Airbnb hire remotely for candidates based in India?

Airbnb does have remote-friendly roles, but Indian candidates applying for positions in the US or Europe will typically need work authorisation in those regions. For roles listed as fully remote without a location restriction, Indian candidates can apply directly. Always check the location tag on the job posting before investing time in an application.

How long does the Airbnb hiring process take from application to offer?

Based on publicly reported candidate experiences, the full process from application to offer commonly takes several weeks. Once the interview loop begins, rounds are typically wrapped up within a couple of weeks. Following up politely with the recruiter after each stage is normal and expected, it signals genuine interest, not impatience.

What salary can I expect at Airbnb?

Compensation varies by role, level, and location. For US-based roles, levels.fyi and Glassdoor carry publicly reported Airbnb pay data you can use as a benchmark before the offer stage. For any India-based roles, Glassdoor India and industry surveys for your specific function are the most relevant reference points. Airbnb is commonly cited as a top-paying employer in the travel-tech category.

Is there a take-home assignment in the Airbnb interview process?

Some roles, particularly in product, design, and certain engineering tracks, include a take-home project or case study as part of the loop. This is not universal across all roles. The recruiter will outline what to expect for your specific position when scheduling the loop. Ask early so you can plan your time without last-minute scrambling.

How important is the Core Values interview compared to technical rounds?

At Airbnb, the values interview is treated as a hard filter, not a courtesy round. Publicly reported candidate experiences consistently mention that strong technical performance does not automatically overcome a weak values interview. Prepare real, specific stories, not polished generalities, for questions about belonging, ownership, and working through disagreement.

Can I apply to multiple Airbnb roles at the same time?

Yes, applying to multiple roles is generally fine, but tailor each application rather than blasting the same CV everywhere. If you are already in process for one role and spot another that is a better fit, let your recruiter know, they can sometimes redirect your candidacy internally. Applying to roles clearly outside your experience level or domain can flag you as an unfocused applicant, so be selective.

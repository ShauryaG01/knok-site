01 Salary Overview

Salary Overview

Data Engineer compensation in India varies by city, company stage, and whether the role is product, platform, or growth-focused. Fixed pay is quoted in LPA (lakhs per annum); variable pay and ESOPs are common at startups. Always clarify fixed vs CTC before negotiating.

04 Negotiation Tips

Negotiation Tips

  1. Anchor with data from 3+ offers or trusted peers at the same level.
  2. Negotiate fixed first; treat variable and ESOP separately.
  3. Ask for signing bonus if base is capped.
  4. Get the offer letter with break-up (basic, HRA, PF) before resigning.
  5. If competing offers exist, share timelines, not numbers, to accelerate decisions.
05 Total Comp Breakdown

Total Comp Breakdown

CTC typically includes: Basic, HRA, special allowance, PF employer contribution, gratuity provision, and projected variable. Ask for in-hand estimate after tax. ESOPs at startups may not appear in CTC, model dilution and vesting cliff separately.

06 Market Snapshot

Market Snapshot

As of 8 July 2026, knok jobradar tracked 74 active Data Engineer openings in Bangalore, making it the city with the most listings for this role anywhere in India. Delhi follows with 60 openings, while Pune (20), Hyderabad (17), Chennai (10), and Mumbai (9) make up the rest of the national picture. Bangalore's lead is no surprise: the city hosts a dense cluster of global capability centres (GCCs), well-funded product startups, and fintech firms that all run data-heavy pipelines. If you want the widest selection of Data Engineer roles in one place, Bangalore is your best bet right now.

09 How To Stand Out

How To Stand Out

Bangalore's Data Engineer market is active, but competition is real. Here is what separates shortlisted candidates from the rest. Get hands-on with the cloud stack employers actually use GCCs like BoschGroup run workloads on AWS, Azure, or GCP. Product companies like Razorpay are heavily AWS-centric. Hands-on experience with at least one major cloud platform, and the ability to discuss cost, scale, and reliability trade-offs, matters more than a long list of tools on your resume. Know your pipeline tools end to end Apache Spark, dbt, Airflow, and Kafka appear repeatedly in Bangalore Data Engineer job descriptions. Interviewers at product companies test whether you can set up and debug these tools, not just name them. Build a portfolio project that goes from raw ingestion to a clean data model and includes at least one scheduled workflow. SQL is still the filter round Every company on this list, from Goldman Sachs to Vegapay, uses a SQL round to screen candidates. Practice window functions, query optimisation, and writing readable, production-quality SQL. Many candidates fail here despite strong Python or Spark skills. Add domain context wherever you can If you have fintech or BFSI experience, lean into it. JPMorgan Chase, Goldman Sachs, Razorpay, and Vegapay all work with finance-adjacent data domains, transactions, reconciliations, fraud signals. Framing your past work in terms of business outcomes ('reduced reconciliation lag' or 'improved data freshness for risk dashboards') lands better than listing tool names alone.

10 Application Strategy

Application Strategy

With 74 openings across Bangalore, being systematic pays off more than applying at random. Prioritise by company type first Decide what you want from this move before you apply. GCCs offer stability and global scope; product companies offer speed and ownership; startups offer breadth and equity. Mixing all three without a clear story makes it hard to tailor your resume or answer 'why us?' convincingly in interviews. Target BoschGroup separately With 10 open roles, BoschGroup is the single biggest opportunity on this list. Check whether the roles span multiple sub-teams, data platform, analytics engineering, or adjacent areas, since you may be able to apply to more than one position with a lightly tailored resume. Apply early in the hiring cycle Most of these openings were posted in July 2026. Response rates drop sharply after the first few weeks of a listing going live, as recruiter queues fill up. If a role looks right, apply promptly rather than spending days perfecting every line of your resume. Message the hiring team directly For product companies and startups, a short, relevant note to the hiring manager or a Data Engineer on the team, via LinkedIn, noticeably improves your chances of reaching a first call. Keep it to a few sentences: who you are, one relevant thing you have built, and what specifically interests you about their data work. Q Questions

Frequently asked

Which type of company is better for a Data Engineer in Bangalore, GCC, product startup, or MNC?

It depends on what you want next. GCCs like JPMorgan Chase and Goldman Sachs give you process maturity, global exposure, and relatively stable pay, good if you want to build depth in a specialised domain. Product companies like Razorpay move faster, give you ownership closer to revenue, and are better for your resume if you plan to stay in the product-startup ecosystem. Startups offer the broadest scope but come with more uncertainty. Most experienced engineers do at least one stint in each category over the course of a career.

What skills do I need to get shortlisted for Data Engineer roles in Bangalore?

The core stack that appears consistently across Bangalore job descriptions includes SQL, Python, at least one cloud platform (AWS, Azure, or GCP), and pipeline tools such as Apache Spark, Airflow, or dbt. Beyond tools, employers want to see that you can design reliable pipelines and explain data quality trade-offs clearly. A portfolio project or a GitHub repo with real ingestion and transformation code is worth more than certifications alone.

Is Bangalore really the best city for Data Engineer jobs compared to Delhi or Hyderabad?

By raw open-role count, yes, Bangalore had 74 openings versus Delhi's 60, Pune's 20, and Hyderabad's 17 as of 8 July 2026. Bangalore also gives you the widest mix of GCC, product, and startup options in one city. That said, Delhi is closing the gap, particularly in BFSI and e-commerce data roles, so it is worth monitoring if your target companies have a strong presence there.

How competitive is the hiring process at companies like Goldman Sachs or Razorpay?

Both are selective. Goldman Sachs typically runs a multi-stage process that includes a coding screen, SQL and data modelling rounds, and a system design discussion focused on large-scale data problems. Razorpay tends to move faster and weights practical pipeline experience heavily, expect a take-home or live coding exercise on real-world data scenarios. In both cases, strong SQL and distributed systems fundamentals are non-negotiable.

Can I negotiate salary for a Data Engineer role in Bangalore, and what is the best approach?

Yes, and you should, especially at GCCs and funded product companies where the initial offer is rarely the final one. Go in knowing the LPA band for your experience level from the salary table above, and bring a competing offer or a verifiable market reference from Glassdoor or levels.fyi if you have one. Always ask for the full CTC breakdown, base, variable, PF, and any RSU or ESOP, before comparing numbers, because two offers at the same headline LPA can look very different once equity and bonus are accounted for.

Are remote or hybrid Data Engineer roles common among Bangalore listings?

Hybrid is now the default at most GCCs and large product companies in Bangalore, with fully remote positions being less common. RemoteStar Team on this list is a notable exception, it explicitly hires for distributed setups. If remote work is a priority, filter for it early in your search, as most listings that say 'Bangalore' expect in-office presence at least part of the week. Startups are sometimes more flexible, but confirm the arrangement before you go deep into interviews.

01 Hiring Overview

Hiring Overview

Okta is one of the world's leading identity and access management companies, trusted by thousands of enterprises worldwide to secure digital identities. Their products, single sign-on, multi-factor authentication, and lifecycle management, sit at the core of how modern organisations control who gets access to what. In India, Okta's engineering, customer success, and sales teams have grown steadily, with the Bengaluru office serving as a major hub. As of July 2026, Okta has 477 open roles globally, spanning software engineering, product management, sales, customer success, solutions engineering, and corporate functions. If you want to work on security infrastructure that millions of people depend on every day, Okta is worth targeting seriously. Competition is real, but the process is structured and predictable once you know what to expect.

03 Interview Process

Interview Process

Okta's hiring typically moves through four to five stages. Exact formats vary by team and role, but here is what most candidates report: Stage 1, Recruiter Screen A brief introductory call with a talent acquisition partner. Expect questions about your current role, notice period, location preference, and compensation expectations. This is mostly a logistics and fit check. Be clear and honest about your situation, recruiters appreciate candidates who know what they want. Stage 2, Hiring Manager or Technical Phone Screen A one-on-one conversation with the hiring manager or a senior team member. For engineering roles, expect light technical questions or a walkthrough of a past project. For non-engineering roles, expect scenario-based questions about your domain experience. This round is typically under an hour and sets the tone for whether you move to the deeper rounds. Stage 3, Technical Assessment (Engineering Roles) Engineering candidates usually receive a take-home coding challenge or complete a timed online assessment. Problems focus on data structures, algorithms, and system design depending on seniority. Non-engineering roles sometimes receive a case study or a short presentation assignment at this stage instead. Stage 4, Virtual Onsite or Panel Loop The most intensive stage. You will meet multiple interviewers, typically a mix of engineers, the hiring manager, and sometimes a product or cross-functional partner. Expect a system design discussion, a couple of coding rounds, and a behavioural interview focused on Okta's values. This entire block is usually scheduled across a single day or across two consecutive days. Stage 5, Offer and Background Check If you clear the panel, a recruiter calls with a verbal offer followed by a written offer letter. Background verification is standard. Okta moves relatively quickly once the panel is complete, candidates commonly report hearing back within a couple of weeks of the final round.

04 What They Look For

What They Look For

Okta looks for a combination of technical depth and clear communication, with an added emphasis on security awareness given the nature of their product. For engineering roles:
- Strong fundamentals in data structures and algorithms
- Experience with distributed systems, cloud infrastructure (AWS, GCP, or Azure), or identity protocols such as OAuth, SAML, and OIDC
- Ability to write clean, maintainable code and articulate design trade-offs clearly
- Awareness of security best practices, even for backend or frontend engineers who are not in a dedicated security team For customer-facing roles (sales, customer success, solutions engineering):
- Understanding of enterprise SaaS and IT buying cycles
- Ability to explain technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders
- Familiarity with identity and access management, even at a conceptual level
- A strong track record in quota attainment or customer retention, depending on the function Across all roles:
- Genuine alignment with Okta's focus on customer success
- Clear written and verbal communication, Okta is a distributed company and writing matters
- A collaborative mindset, panels specifically probe how you handle disagreement and cross-team situations

05 How To Prepare

How To Prepare

1. Understand Okta's product firsthand
Create a free Okta developer account and explore how single sign-on, multi-factor authentication, and lifecycle management actually work. Interviewers consistently appreciate candidates who have used the product rather than just read about it. 2. Brush up on identity protocols
Even if you are not applying for a security-specific role, understanding OAuth 2.0, SAML, and OpenID Connect at a conceptual level will impress interviewers and help you ask sharper questions during your rounds. 3. Practise system design with a security lens
For engineering roles, be ready to design systems where access control, token management, and audit logging are first-class concerns, not afterthoughts. Okta interviewers notice when candidates treat security as a bolt-on. 4. Prepare STAR stories tied to Okta's values
Okta's behavioural rounds focus on customer obsession, transparency, and cross-functional collaboration. Prepare two or three stories for each theme using the Situation-Task-Action-Result format before you walk into the panel. 5. Research Okta's recent direction
Read their investor updates, blog posts, and product announcements from 2024 to 2026. Being able to reference a recent product launch or strategic move signals genuine interest and separates you from candidates who only skimmed the 'About' page. 6. Benchmark your salary expectations
Before the recruiter screen, look up compensation ranges on Glassdoor and levels.fyi for Okta India roles so you can state a confident, realistic number rather than leaving it vague. 7. Let technology handle the search

06 Culture And Values

Culture And Values

Okta's culture is anchored in a set of values the company calls the 'Okta Way.' The most prominent pillars are: - Love our customers, every decision, from product to engineering, is expected to trace back to customer impact
- Never stop innovating, incremental improvement is valued, but so is the willingness to challenge existing approaches
- Act with integrity, particularly relevant given Okta's role in enterprise security; employees are expected to be transparent about mistakes and escalate concerns early
- Be transparent, internal communication tends to be open; teams share context freely rather than working in silos
- Empower with trust, Okta's own product is about enabling trust, and that philosophy extends internally to how managers engage with their teams Glassdoor reviews from employees in India frequently mention a fast-paced but collaborative environment with strong learning opportunities in the cloud security space. Okta has faced publicly reported security incidents in recent years, which by all publicly reported accounts has led to significant internal investment in both product hardening and a culture of greater accountability. If this topic comes up in your interview, acknowledging it honestly and showing curiosity about how the company responded will reflect well on you.

Q Questions

Frequently asked

How many open roles does Okta have right now?

As of July 2026, Okta has 477 open roles globally. These span engineering, product, sales, customer success, and corporate functions. The Bengaluru office is a significant hiring centre, especially for engineering and support roles. Check Okta's careers page regularly as roles are added frequently and some close quickly.

What CTC can I expect from Okta India?

Okta does not publish salary bands publicly for India roles. Based on data shared by employees on Glassdoor and levels.fyi, compensation at Okta India is commonly cited as competitive with other US-headquartered product companies operating here. Research role-specific ranges on those platforms before your recruiter screen so you can give a confident, informed number rather than leaving it open-ended.

Does Okta hire freshers or new graduates?

Okta occasionally runs internship and new-graduate programmes, but the majority of their 477 open roles target candidates with prior industry experience. If you are a fresher, look specifically for roles labelled 'Associate' or check their campus recruiting page. Building side projects that demonstrate knowledge of identity protocols or cloud security can meaningfully strengthen your profile.

How long does Okta's full hiring process usually take?

Most candidates report the complete process, from recruiter screen to written offer, taking a few weeks to about a month, though timelines vary by team and urgency. Engineering roles with a take-home assessment can extend this slightly. Stay proactive: follow up with your recruiter after each stage if you have not heard back within a week, as pipelines can stall simply due to scheduling.

Are Okta's India roles remote, hybrid, or in-office?

Okta follows a hybrid model for most India-based roles, with the Bengaluru office as the primary hub. The exact in-office requirement varies by team and seniority. Role descriptions on the careers page usually specify whether a position is remote-friendly, hybrid, or office-based, so read the job description carefully, do not assume either way before applying.

What should I emphasise if I am coming from a non-security background?

Okta hires broadly, not every role requires a security specialist. Emphasise transferable strengths: distributed systems experience for backend engineers, enterprise SaaS experience for sales and customer success candidates, and data platform skills for analytics roles. Show that you have taken the time to understand what identity and access management means for businesses, even at a high level, and you will already stand out from most applicants who have not done that homework.

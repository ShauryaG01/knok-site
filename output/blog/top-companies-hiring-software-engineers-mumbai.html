01 Salary Overview

Salary Overview

Software Engineer compensation in India varies by city, company stage, and whether the role is product, platform, or growth-focused. Fixed pay is quoted in LPA (lakhs per annum); variable pay and ESOPs are common at startups. Always clarify fixed vs CTC before negotiating.

03 By City

By City

Bangalore / Hyderabad: Highest volume of tech roles; senior Software Engineer bands at top of range.
Mumbai / Delhi NCR: Strong fintech and consumer; premiums for leadership roles.
Pune / Chennai: GCC and enterprise SaaS; competitive base, lower equity upside.
Mumbai: Factor in cost of living vs Bangalore when comparing offers.

04 Negotiation Tips

Negotiation Tips

  1. Anchor with data from 3+ offers or trusted peers at the same level.
  2. Negotiate fixed first; treat variable and ESOP separately.
  3. Ask for signing bonus if base is capped.
  4. Get the offer letter with break-up (basic, HRA, PF) before resigning.
  5. If competing offers exist, share timelines, not numbers, to accelerate decisions.
05 Total Comp Breakdown

Total Comp Breakdown

CTC typically includes: Basic, HRA, special allowance, PF employer contribution, gratuity provision, and projected variable. Ask for in-hand estimate after tax. ESOPs at startups may not appear in CTC, model dilution and vesting cliff separately.

06 Market Snapshot

Market Snapshot

Mumbai had 65 Software Engineer openings as of 8 July 2026 (knok jobradar). That puts it well behind Bangalore (684 roles) and the other major hubs, Delhi (129), Hyderabad (130), and Pune (124), but ahead of Chennai (43). What makes Mumbai stand out is *who* is hiring: the city's financial-capital status draws global banks, financial-data firms, and fintech startups, giving the job market a noticeably different flavour from the pure-tech hubs. If you are open to Pune (124 roles, just a short drive away), your options nearly double overnight.

09 How To Stand Out

How To Stand Out

Mumbai's hiring mix is heavily skewed toward finance and fintech. These tips are calibrated for that reality. 1. Show domain awareness, Candidates who understand basic financial concepts (market data, trading, risk) get a second look at Morningstar, JPMorgan Chase, imc, and Nasdaq, even for pure-engineering roles. Add a line to your resume if you have any exposure. 2. Tailor your resume per company type, GCCs prize scale, reliability, and process; Indian startups want ownership, speed, and product thinking. Keep two versions ready. 3. Highlight system-design experience, Financial-data and payments systems demand low-latency, high-availability design. Mention any experience with distributed systems, message queues, or real-time data pipelines. 4. Build a visible GitHub or portfolio, Startups like Anaira AI and IDfy often check GitHub before the HR call. Even one solid project matters. 5. Get LinkedIn right, Most Mumbai GCC recruiters source candidates directly on LinkedIn. Set 'Open to Work' visible to recruiters, add your current location as Mumbai, and keep your skills section updated.

10 Application Strategy

Application Strategy

Step 1, Target by type first
Decide whether you want the stability of a GCC or the growth pace of a startup. Your resume, prep, and salary expectations will differ meaningfully between the two tracks. Step 2, Apply early
Roles at Morningstar (11 open) and JPMorgan Chase (9 open) attract high volumes. Applications submitted in the first few days after a role is posted have a noticeably better chance of being seen by a recruiter. Step 3, Reach out to HR directly
For Indian product companies, haptik, webengage, upGrad, myglamm, IDfy, a short, specific LinkedIn message to the hiring manager or recruiter can move you ahead of the queue. Mention the exact role and one relevant project. Step 4, Track Pune in parallel
Pune has 124 Software Engineer openings. If you are willing to relocate, or if your current company has a Pune office, keeping an eye on both cities significantly widens your options. Step 5, Use automation for the volume work

Q Questions

Frequently asked

Which company in Mumbai has the most Software Engineer openings right now?

Morningstar leads with 11 open roles, followed by JPMorgan Chase with 9 and imc with 8 (knok jobradar, 8 July 2026). All three are global firms with GCC-style operations in Mumbai, meaning they tend to hire in batches and keep roles open longer than startups do. If you are targeting any of these, applying sooner rather than later is advisable.

Is Mumbai a good city for Software Engineers compared to Bangalore?

Bangalore dominates with 684 roles versus Mumbai's 65, so in raw volume there is no comparison. However, Mumbai offers something Bangalore does not: deep access to global finance and fintech employers like JPMorgan Chase, Morningstar, and Nasdaq. If you want a career at the intersection of engineering and financial services, Mumbai is one of the best cities in India for that path.

What salary should a mid-level Software Engineer expect in Mumbai?

The typical range for 3-5 years of experience is 15-25 LPA (knok jobradar data). Glassdoor and levels.fyi publicly report that top-tier fintech and banking GCCs in Mumbai can go higher for strong candidates, especially those with domain knowledge in finance or trading. Indian product companies like webengage and upGrad are increasingly competitive in this range as well.

What is a GCC and why does it matter for my job search in Mumbai?

GCC stands for Global Capability Centre, an in-house tech and operations unit set up by a multinational company in India. Mumbai has a strong GCC presence from financial firms like Morningstar, JPMorgan Chase, and Nasdaq. GCC roles generally offer structured compensation, clearer career ladders, and exposure to global teams, but may move slower than a startup in terms of product ownership and pace of promotion.

Should I apply to startups like haptik or Anaira AI, or focus on big names?

It depends on what you are optimising for. Startups like haptik (conversational AI) and Anaira AI offer faster growth, broader ownership of problems, and a chance to build something from early stages. Established names like JPMorgan Chase and Morningstar offer stronger brand value on your resume, more predictable compensation, and larger team structures. Many engineers in Mumbai pursue both tracks simultaneously and let the interview process decide.

How do I get noticed by recruiters at Mumbai's top tech companies?

For GCCs, LinkedIn is the primary sourcing tool, set your profile to 'Open to Work' (visible to recruiters), list Mumbai as your location, and keep your skills section current. For startups, a direct message to the hiring manager citing the exact role and one relevant project works well. At companies like IDfy and myglamm, a visible GitHub profile or a portfolio link in your resume significantly increases callback rates.

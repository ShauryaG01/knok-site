01 Salary Overview

Salary Overview

Walk into most Data Engineer interviews in Delhi NCR and the salary is rarely discussed before the final round. Most Indian job listings skip the pay range entirely, or drop in a vague line like 'as per industry standards.' That tells you nothing useful when you are planning your next move.

knok jobradar tracked 63 active Data Engineer openings in Delhi NCR as of 2026-07-08. By combining listing signals with publicly reported offer data and Glassdoor figures, we built the salary bands in this guide. They reflect what real candidates in Delhi NCR are seeing in offer letters right now, not what companies advertise.

The short version: Data Engineers in Delhi NCR earn 6-12 LPA at entry level and 42-65+ LPA at lead or staff level. The gap between levels is steep because demand for senior pipeline and platform skills continues to outpace supply, particularly in fintech, e-commerce, and cloud-first product companies concentrated in Noida, Gurgaon, and the broader NCR region.

04 Negotiation Tips

Negotiation Tips

Salary negotiation for Data Engineers in India is less awkward than most people expect, because companies know this skill is in demand.

  1. Anchor on market bands, not just your current CTC. Most recruiters open with 'what is your current package?' Sharing it is optional. You can say 'I am looking for something in the 28-45 LPA range based on what the market shows for this level,' and point to publicly reported data as your source.
  1. Separate fixed from variable early. An offer with a large variable component tied to bonus targets can look great on paper but deliver less in practice. Ask explicitly what percentage of CTC is guaranteed before you compare two offers.
  1. Name your specialisation. If you work with Databricks, Kafka, or real-time streaming rather than basic batch ETL, say so clearly when negotiating. Industry surveys consistently show a premium for real-time and lakehouse-stack engineers versus general ETL profiles.
  1. Wait for the written offer before countering. Verbal offers shift. Once you have a written letter, you are negotiating from a stable position and have something to reference line by line.
  1. Counter once, clearly, in writing. A short email with a specific number and a brief reason (competing offer, market data, your specialisation) lands better than a phone call where the recruiter can walk you in circles.
05 Total Comp Breakdown

Total Comp Breakdown

Indian offer letters bundle several components under one CTC number. Understanding what is inside helps you compare two offers accurately rather than just comparing the headline figure.

Fixed pay (basic + HRA + allowances): This is the take-home-adjacent portion of your salary. It makes up a large share of CTC at entry and mid level. Always check what percentage of CTC this is before signing.

Variable pay / performance bonus: Paid quarterly or annually depending on the company. At senior levels, this is commonly cited at 10 to 20 percent of CTC. Ask for the last two years of payout history to judge whether targets are realistic or routinely missed.

ESOPs / RSUs: More common at funded startups and listed tech companies than at IT service firms. At Lead/Staff level (42-65+ LPA CTC), stock can push effective annual earnings well above the base CTC over a vesting period. Treat this as potential upside, not guaranteed income.

Provident Fund (PF): Employer PF contribution is included in CTC by many companies but goes into your PF account, not your bank account. Factor this in when comparing offers side by side.

Gratuity: Usually included in CTC calculations. Payable only after completing the statutory minimum tenure of continuous service, so it matters more for roles you plan to stay in long term.

Q Questions

Frequently asked

Is 12 LPA a good salary for a fresher Data Engineer in Delhi NCR?

The entry band runs 6-12 LPA, so 12 LPA sits at the top of what freshers typically see in Delhi NCR. At a product company or a well-funded startup, top-of-band offers are achievable with strong SQL and Python skills plus a relevant project or internship. At IT service companies, the lower end of the band is more common for freshers, with increments kicking in after the first performance cycle.

How different is Bangalore pay from Delhi NCR for Data Engineers?

Bangalore had 84 open roles versus Delhi NCR's 63 in the knok jobradar scan. Glassdoor commonly cited figures suggest senior packages in Bangalore can run slightly higher. The gap at mid-level is smaller. If you factor in cost of living, Delhi NCR (particularly Noida) can feel comparable net-of-rent to Bangalore for many professionals making the comparison.

What skills push a Data Engineer from mid-level to senior pay?

The jump from the 14-26 LPA band to the 28-45 LPA band usually comes from owning full pipelines end-to-end, not just writing scripts others design. Senior engineers are expected to architect for scale and reliability, handle stakeholder conversations, and mentor junior team members. Specialisations in real-time streaming (Kafka, Flink) or cloud-native lakehouses (Databricks, BigQuery) are publicly reported as carrying a meaningful premium in offer negotiations.

What percentage of CTC should be fixed pay?

There is no single rule, but industry surveys suggest most mid-to-senior engineers aim for at least 70-75 percent of CTC as fixed. At entry level, the fixed proportion is typically higher. Watch out for offers where variable pay is large but tied to company-wide targets you cannot directly influence. Ask for recent payout history before accepting a high-variable structure.

Do Data Engineers in Delhi NCR get ESOPs?

ESOPs are more common at funded product startups than at IT service firms. At Lead/Staff level (42-65+ LPA CTC), stock can meaningfully increase total earnings over a vesting period, and levels.fyi publicly reports stock as a significant component for senior hires at Indian product companies. At entry and mid level, stock grants are smaller and less common unless the company is pre-IPO and using equity to partially offset cash.

How often do Delhi NCR companies negotiate on salary?

Negotiation is widely accepted and expected in Indian tech hiring. Publicly reported recruiter feedback from India consistently shows that candidates who counter with a specific number and a clear reason (market data, competing offer, or specialisation) receive a revised offer in most cases. The key is to counter once, in writing, rather than going back and forth multiple times, which can stall the process.

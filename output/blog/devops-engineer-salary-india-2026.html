01 Salary Overview

Salary Overview

## DevOps Engineer Salary in India (2026)

The pay transparency problem comes first. Most Indian job listings still do not show a salary range. Of the 746 DevOps Engineer openings tracked by knok jobradar as of 8 July 2026, only a small fraction display any compensation figure — which means candidates are negotiating blind and often under-value themselves at the offer stage.

Here is what the data does tell us. Salaries for DevOps Engineers in India span a wide range — from around ₹6 LPA for a fresher stepping into a first role, all the way past ₹70 LPA for a Lead or Staff-level engineer at a product company or MNC. The spread is not random: experience, city, cloud specialisation, and company type each pull the number in a distinct direction.

The salary bands in this article are drawn from the knok jobradar dataset (source: 746 active listings as of 2026-07-08). Where the data is thin, we say so rather than guess.

04 Negotiation Tips

Negotiation Tips

## How to Negotiate Your DevOps Offer

1. Anchor to the band, not the job listing
Because most listings hide salary, you may not know the range before an offer arrives. Use the experience bands in this article as your anchor. If you are a 4-year DevOps engineer with Kubernetes production ownership, ₹15 – ₹28 LPA is your legitimate reference point — say it out loud.

2. Separate base from total comp
Ask for the offer letter to itemise: base salary, variable bonus, joining bonus (if any), stock or ESOP, and allowances. A high base is generally better than a high variable unless the bonus is guaranteed in writing.

3. Certifications are real leverage
AWS Solutions Architect, CKA, or Terraform Associate certifications are publicly recognised pay differentiators in this role. If you hold one, name it explicitly during negotiation — it is not bragging, it is market evidence that your skills command a premium.

4. Counter at least once
Industry surveys consistently find that candidates who make a single counter-offer receive a higher final package than those who accept the first number. The worst answer you can get is no.

5. Mention competing offers ethically
If you have another offer, you can reference it without sharing the exact figure. 'I have a competing offer in a similar range and would like to know if there is flexibility here' is professional and effective.

6. Time your ask correctly
Negotiate after the verbal offer, before signing. Trying to renegotiate after you have signed damages trust and rarely succeeds.

Q Questions

Frequently asked

What is the starting salary for a DevOps Engineer fresher in India in 2026?

The entry band for DevOps Engineers with 0-2 years of experience runs from ₹6 LPA to ₹12 LPA based on current active listings. Candidates who bring a cloud certification or a Kubernetes credential typically land closer to the top of that range. Pure freshers without certifications or any hands-on project work generally start at the lower end. Building even one production-style project on GitHub before applying makes a visible difference to interviewers.

Does city matter that much for DevOps salaries in India?

City matters, but company type matters more. A senior DevOps engineer at a product company in Hyderabad will often earn more than one at a services firm in Bangalore. That said, Bangalore has 165 active openings right now versus 10 in Mumbai — sheer volume means more high-paying roles are available there. Glassdoor data suggests city premiums exist but are narrowing as remote and hybrid work becomes more common. If you are targeting a specific company rather than a city, follow the company.

How do I move from the mid band (₹15-28 LPA) to the senior band (₹30-50 LPA)?

The jump almost always requires evidence of production ownership — not just running tools, but owning reliability outcomes. Interviewers at the senior level want to hear about incidents you led, on-call rotations you shaped, or platform migrations you drove end to end. Getting a second cloud certification or deepening Terraform and Kubernetes expertise helps, but the scope-of-responsibility story is what actually unlocks senior compensation. Many engineers make this jump by switching companies rather than waiting for an internal promotion cycle.

Are DevOps salaries different at startups versus MNCs in India?

Yes, structurally different. MNCs tend to offer more predictable base salaries, stronger health benefits, and structured appraisal cycles — but the ceiling on base pay is often lower than at a well-funded startup. Funded startups may offer a lower base paired with ESOPs that could be worth a lot if the company does well — or nothing if it does not. Industry surveys suggest mid-to-late-stage startups increasingly match MNC base salaries while adding equity on top. Always ask a startup how their ESOP scheme works and what the current valuation basis is before you compare offers side by side.

Which DevOps skills command the highest pay in 2026?

Platform engineering, FinOps (cloud cost optimisation), and security-integrated DevOps (often called DevSecOps) are the three areas where publicly reported premiums are most often cited right now. Kubernetes at scale and multi-cloud architecture experience also consistently appear in senior and Lead/Staff listings. Skills tied to AI infrastructure — GPU cluster management and ML pipeline tooling — are newer but already visible in the highest-paying job descriptions. The safest investment is deep expertise in one cloud provider plus Kubernetes, then layering a specialty on top.

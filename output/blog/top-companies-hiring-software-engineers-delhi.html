01 Salary Overview

Salary Overview

Software Engineer compensation in India varies by city, company stage, and whether the role is product, platform, or growth-focused. Fixed pay is quoted in LPA (lakhs per annum); variable pay and ESOPs are common at startups. Always clarify fixed vs CTC before negotiating.

04 Negotiation Tips

Negotiation Tips

  1. Anchor with data from 3+ offers or trusted peers at the same level.
  2. Negotiate fixed first; treat variable and ESOP separately.
  3. Ask for signing bonus if base is capped.
  4. Get the offer letter with break-up (basic, HRA, PF) before resigning.
  5. If competing offers exist, share timelines, not numbers, to accelerate decisions.
05 Total Comp Breakdown

Total Comp Breakdown

CTC typically includes: Basic, HRA, special allowance, PF employer contribution, gratuity provision, and projected variable. Ask for in-hand estimate after tax. ESOPs at startups may not appear in CTC, model dilution and vesting cliff separately.

06 Market Snapshot

Market Snapshot

Delhi NCR had 171 Software Engineer openings on 2026-07-08, according to knok jobradar. That places it solidly in the middle of India's major tech hubs, Bangalore leads with 958 openings, Hyderabad has 183, Pune has 169, and Mumbai has 81. Delhi NCR punches above its weight when you look at employer quality rather than just raw volume. What makes the Delhi NCR market stand out is its mix: Global Capability Centres (GCCs) from large multinationals, mature product companies with real engineering cultures, and funded startups all hiring at the same time. Whether you want the stability of a big corporate name or the speed of a ten-person team, this market has something for you.

09 How To Stand Out

How To Stand Out

With 171 openings across a diverse employer set, the candidates who land interviews are the ones who tailor their approach to the type of company, not just the job title. For GCCs (Deutsche Telekom Digital Labs): Highlight work on large, distributed systems, compliance-aware engineering, or enterprise integrations. Use precise technical language, these teams review a lot of resumes and want specifics. For product companies (GreyOrange, Wingify, Anaplan, Payoneer): Show ownership. Talk about features you built end-to-end, metrics you moved, and bugs you caught before users did. 'I worked on the payments module' is weaker than 'I reduced checkout failures by optimising retry logic.' For startups (Wishlink, MobiKwik): Signal versatility. Mention instances where you handled responsibilities outside your core role, setting up a CI/CD pipeline, onboarding a junior developer, or picking a tech stack from scratch. Across the board: Mirror the language in each job description. ATS systems filter out resumes that do not contain the right keywords before a human ever reads them. If you genuinely have the skill, name it the way they named it.

10 Application Strategy

Application Strategy

Delhi NCR's 171 openings are spread across several employer types, so a targeted approach beats sending the same resume everywhere. 1. Decide your priority lane first. Stability (GCC), product impact (product companies), or growth potential (startups)? This determines which 10 to 15 roles deserve your best effort.
2. Apply early. Companies like GreyOrange and Payoneer have a small number of openings and will close them quickly. Applying in the first few days of a posting is meaningfully better than applying two weeks later.
3. Follow up on LinkedIn. After submitting, send the hiring manager or recruiter a short, specific note referencing the role and one skill that matches their requirements. It takes five minutes and moves your application up the stack.
4. Track everything. When you are running several active applications at once, knowing the status of each one, who you have heard from, who needs a follow-up, which round you are in, is the difference between a managed search and a stressful one. Q Questions

Frequently asked

How does Delhi NCR compare to Bangalore for Software Engineers?

Bangalore has significantly more openings (958 vs 171 in Delhi NCR as of 2026-07-08), but Delhi NCR is a genuine market, not a backup option. The employer mix here includes GCCs, funded product companies, and established fintechs, so the quality of roles is solid. If relocating to Bangalore is off the table for family or lifestyle reasons, Delhi NCR gives you a strong pipeline without compromising on company reputation.

Which companies are best for freshers in Delhi NCR?

Deutsche Telekom Digital Labs (19 openings) and IT Engineering (5 openings) are most likely to run structured fresher or graduate programmes, since larger organisations typically have dedicated hiring tracks for entry-level engineers. Harappa (5 openings), as an EdTech platform, may also have engineering roles suited to freshers who are comfortable with fast-moving product teams. When applying, look for job descriptions that mention mentorship, onboarding programmes, or graduate hiring, these signal that the company has genuine infrastructure for developing junior engineers.

Do product companies pay more than GCCs in Delhi NCR?

The base salary bands often overlap, but the total compensation picture differs. GCCs like Deutsche Telekom Digital Labs tend to offer more predictable structures, reliable increments, good benefits, and clear bands. Product companies like Wingify or Payoneer may offer stronger variable pay and ESOPs that can push total compensation above the GCC equivalent, but only if the equity vests and has value. For actual numbers by company, Glassdoor and levels.fyi are the best cross-references before you negotiate.

What tech skills are most in demand across these top employers?

The employer mix spans telecom GCC, warehouse robotics, SaaS planning, and fintech payments, so the in-demand stack is deliberately broad. That said, roles at GCCs and larger product companies commonly cite Java, Python, and major cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure). Startups like Wishlink and MobiKwik tend to value full-stack versatility over deep specialisation. The safest approach is to read each job description individually, since the required skills vary significantly even within the same 'Software Engineer' title across these ten employers.

Should I apply to Lateral - APAC if I want a permanent role?

Lateral - APAC is a staffing firm, so their 3 openings most likely represent placements at client companies rather than in-house engineering positions. These roles can still lead to permanent employment, contract-to-hire arrangements are common in the Delhi NCR market. Before you invest time in multiple interview rounds, ask upfront whether the specific role is a contract placement, a direct hire, or a contract-to-hire arrangement. That one question saves a lot of confusion later.

How do I get noticed at a company like Wingify or GreyOrange?

Both are relatively tight-knit product teams where a personalised application stands out more than it would at a large GCC. For Wingify, try their product (VWO) and mention something specific about a feature or experiment type that caught your attention. For GreyOrange, reading one of their engineering blogs or thinking through the challenges of real-time warehouse robotics gives you something concrete to reference in your cover note or LinkedIn message. Generic applications rarely rise to the top at companies where the hiring team knows every engineer by name.

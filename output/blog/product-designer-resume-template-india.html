01 Why This Format Works

Why This Format Works

Indian recruiters spend 6-10 seconds on first pass. A Product Designer resume must surface level, domain, and one quantified win in the header. One page for <8 years experience; two pages only for staff+ with patents or large-scope leadership.

02 Resume Structure

Resume Structure

Header: Name, phone, email, LinkedIn, city.
Summary: 2 lines, level + domain + flagship metric.
Experience: Reverse chronological; 3-5 bullets per role with metrics.
Skills: Group by category (tools, domains, methods).
Education: Degree, institute, year, keep brief for experienced candidates.

04 Ats Tips

Ats Tips

Use standard section headings (Experience, Education). Avoid tables and graphics. Mirror JD keywords naturally in bullets. Export PDF from Google Docs or Word, not Canva templates that break parsing.

05 Common Mistakes

Common Mistakes

Listing responsibilities without outcomes; including photo for tech roles; unexplained employment gaps; generic 'hard worker' summaries; skills section bloated with every tool ever used.

Q Questions

Frequently asked

How long should a Product Designer resume be in India?

Keep it to one page for anyone with under eight years of experience, Indian recruiters spend six to ten seconds on the first pass, so density and clarity beat length. Only go to two pages for staff-plus candidates with patents, publications, or multi-team leadership scope worth detailing. If you're padding to fill a second page, you almost certainly shouldn't.

Do Indian ATS systems parse PDF resumes correctly?

Yes, as long as the PDF is exported from Word or Google Docs with real, selectable text and standard section headings like Experience and Education. Avoid Canva or design-heavy templates that use tables, columns, and graphics, parsers frequently garble them, dropping your experience entirely. Mirror the job description's keywords naturally in your bullets so the ATS keyword match actually fires.

What makes a strong resume bullet?

Lead with the outcome and quantify it. Instead of 'responsible for onboarding', write 'cut onboarding from seven steps to three, lifting activation 22% in six weeks'. Recruiters skim for numbers, revenue, retention, latency, cost, or scale, so every bullet should pair an action with a measurable result, and the top two bullets of each role should be tailored to the job you're applying for.

Should I include a photo, and what about personal details?

Skip the photo for tech roles in India unless a posting explicitly asks for one, it adds no signal and can introduce bias. You also don't need date of birth, marital status, or a full address; a city, phone number, email, and LinkedIn URL are enough. Use the reclaimed space for one more quantified achievement instead.

How do I explain an employment gap on my resume?

Be brief and honest rather than hiding it. A short, factual line, upskilling, a certification, caregiving, a health break, or freelance work, reassures recruiters far more than an unexplained gap, which invites worst-case assumptions. If you did anything relevant during the break, such as a course or a side project, list it so the time reads as productive.

01 Salary Overview

Salary Overview

Software Engineer compensation in India varies by city, company stage, and whether the role is product, platform, or growth-focused. Fixed pay is quoted in LPA (lakhs per annum); variable pay and ESOPs are common at startups. Always clarify fixed vs CTC before negotiating.

04 Negotiation Tips

Negotiation Tips

  1. Anchor with data from 3+ offers or trusted peers at the same level.
  2. Negotiate fixed first; treat variable and ESOP separately.
  3. Ask for signing bonus if base is capped.
  4. Get the offer letter with break-up (basic, HRA, PF) before resigning.
  5. If competing offers exist, share timelines, not numbers, to accelerate decisions.
05 Total Comp Breakdown

Total Comp Breakdown

CTC typically includes: Basic, HRA, special allowance, PF employer contribution, gratuity provision, and projected variable. Ask for in-hand estimate after tax. ESOPs at startups may not appear in CTC, model dilution and vesting cliff separately.

06 Market Snapshot

Market Snapshot

Software Engineer Jobs in Hyderabad, Who Is Hiring Right Now As of 8 July 2026, knok jobradar is tracking 183 Software Engineer openings in Hyderabad. That places the city firmly among India's top tech hiring markets, Bangalore leads nationally with 958 openings, but Hyderabad (183) holds its own ahead of Pune (169) and Chennai (75), and is neck-and-neck with Delhi (171). What makes Hyderabad stand out is the mix of employers. HITEC City and Gachibowli host a dense cluster of Global Capability Centres (GCCs), Indian engineering arms of global multinationals, alongside home-grown product companies and established IT services firms. This three-way mix means there are genuine opportunities across experience levels, tech stacks, and salary brackets, all within the same city.

09 How To Stand Out

How To Stand Out

How to Stand Out When Applying 1 Frame your resume for the employer type. GCCs like Cigna or JPMorgan Chase want engineers who can work in large, cross-functional global teams. Product companies like Freshworks want someone who owns features end-to-end. One generic resume will not serve both equally, keep two versions ready. 2. Lead with impact, not just tools. 'Reduced API latency and cut error rates' backed with real data from your work is far more powerful than a list of frameworks. GCC interviewers in particular look for engineers who can articulate business impact alongside technical depth. 3. Prepare for structured coding rounds. JPMorgan Chase and State Street Corporation both run FAANG-style technical screens. If these are on your target list, set aside dedicated time for problem-solving practice before you apply. 4. Know the product before the interview. For Freshworks and Cendyn, use, or at minimum research, their product before you walk into the interview. Product company interviewers notice immediately when a candidate has actually thought about the user experience. 5. Tap your Hyderabad network. HITEC City has a strong community of engineering meetups and alumni circles. A referral at JPMorgan Chase or Warner Bros Discovery can get your resume past the initial screen far faster than a cold online application.

10 Application Strategy

Application Strategy

How to Apply Without Spending All Day on Job Boards With 183 openings spread across GCCs, product companies, and IT services firms, the challenge is not finding jobs, it is keeping up before roles fill. Here is a practical plan: Start with the top-hiring companies directly. Cigna (14 roles), Amgen Inc (11), JPMorgan Chase (10), and Warner Bros Discovery (9) are the most active right now. Go to their careers pages and set up job alerts so you hear about new listings the day they go up. Tailor your resume for each employer type. One version emphasising scale, process, and cross-team collaboration for GCCs; another emphasising ownership and shipping speed for product companies. A recruiter at Freshworks and a recruiter at State Street Corporation are looking for quite different signals. Message hiring managers, not just HR. Many Hyderabad GCC roles fill through referrals before a public posting even closes. A short, respectful LinkedIn note to the right engineering manager, rather than a generic apply-button click, can move you ahead of the queue. Q Questions

Frequently asked

Is Hyderabad a good city for Software Engineer jobs in 2026?

Yes, knok jobradar shows 183 Software Engineer openings in Hyderabad as of July 2026, making it one of the top hiring markets outside Bangalore. The city has a healthy mix of GCCs, product companies, and IT services firms, so there are roles suited to engineers at every level and career preference. Hyderabad's cost of living is publicly reported to be lower than Bangalore's, which makes the effective value of a given LPA salary meaningfully higher here.

Which type of company pays more in Hyderabad, GCC or product company?

At the senior and lead levels, GCCs like JPMorgan Chase and State Street Corporation typically pay toward the top of the ₹28-₹45 LPA and ₹40-₹65+ LPA ranges. Product companies like Freshworks may offer a slightly lower fixed salary but compensate with ESOPs that can be valuable if the company grows. For fresher and mid-level engineers, the difference is smaller and varies significantly by role and interview performance.

Which Hyderabad companies are hiring the most Software Engineers right now?

As of 8 July 2026, Cigna leads with 14 open roles, followed by Amgen Inc (11), JPMorgan Chase (10), and Warner Bros Discovery (9). Infosys, Cornerstone, and Cendyn each have 6 openings, while Freshworks and State Street Corporation have 5 each. Job counts shift daily as new roles are posted and old ones close, so it is worth checking fresh data before you apply.

Do Hyderabad GCCs hire freshers or only experienced engineers?

Most GCCs do hire freshers, especially for associate software engineer or graduate trainee programmes. The entry-level salary band of ₹6-₹12 LPA reflects this fresher market. However, GCCs like JPMorgan Chase and State Street run structured assessments, coding tests and multiple interview rounds, even for entry-level roles, so strong preparation matters. IT services firms like Infosys typically have a higher fresher intake with a more structured onboarding and training programme.

How long does it take to hear back after applying to these companies?

Timelines vary widely by employer type. GCCs like JPMorgan Chase often run multi-stage processes that can stretch over several weeks from application to offer. Product companies like Freshworks tend to move faster, especially for mid-level roles. Applying through a referral consistently shortens timelines at both types of companies, since your resume reaches the right hiring manager rather than waiting in a general application pool.

Is it worth applying to multiple Hyderabad companies at the same time?

Absolutely, parallel applications are standard practice in Indian tech hiring. Cigna, JPMorgan Chase, and Freshworks all run independent pipelines, so being in multiple processes simultaneously creates no conflict and gives you leverage when comparing offers. The main challenge is tracking deadlines, follow-ups, and interview schedules across several companies at once, which is where automating the application stage frees up your time for the rounds that actually matter.

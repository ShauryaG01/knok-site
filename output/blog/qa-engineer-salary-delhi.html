01 Salary Overview

Salary Overview

QA Engineer Salary in Delhi NCR (2026)

Here is an honest starting point: most job listings in India do not show salary. Companies post 'as per industry standards' or leave the field blank, which makes it genuinely difficult to know what to expect before you apply. The numbers below come from knok jobradar data pulled on July 8, 2026, covering 65 active QA Engineer openings in Delhi NCR.

Across all experience levels, QA Engineers in Delhi NCR earn anywhere from 4 LPA at the start of their career to 45+ LPA in a senior lead role. The range is wide because 'QA Engineer' covers a lot of ground: a manual tester doing basic regression work sits at one end, while a senior automation architect owning a full test platform sits at the other.

If you are preparing for an interview or offer negotiation, the most useful thing is knowing which experience band your skills and the company's size place you in. The sections below break that down.

Salary by Experience Level

Entry (0-2 years, 4-9 LPA): Most roles at this stage focus on manual testing, writing test cases, and logging bugs. If you come in with a scripting skill such as Python or basic Selenium, or a relevant certification, you can push toward the higher end of this band even as a fresher.

Mid (3-5 years, 9-17 LPA): This is where automation skills start to matter a lot. Engineers who can write and maintain test scripts, set up CI pipelines, and work with tools like Selenium, Appium, or Cypress tend to land offers in the upper half of this band. Those still doing primarily manual work generally sit lower.

Senior (6-9 years, 17-30 LPA): At this level, companies expect you to own a testing strategy, mentor junior engineers, and integrate QA into the development pipeline. Specialisations in performance testing, security testing, or mobile automation push salaries toward the top of this range.

Lead (9+ years, 28-45+ LPA): Lead and principal QA roles involve hiring decisions, architecture choices, and direct stakeholder communication. The overlap with the Senior band at the lower end reflects that 'Lead' means very different things at different companies.

Listings won't tell you what a role pays, but knok will tell you which ones you're competitive for.

04 Negotiation Tips

Negotiation Tips

How to Negotiate Your QA Engineer Offer

Know your automation stack's value. Manual QA and automation QA are priced differently by most hiring teams. If you have hands-on experience with Selenium, Pytest, Cypress, Playwright, or a CI tool like Jenkins or GitHub Actions, that is a concrete reason to anchor higher within your band. Name the tools explicitly when you negotiate, do not just say 'I do automation.'

Use competing offers, not just salary data. Recruiters respond more to 'I have another offer at X LPA' than to 'the market says X LPA.' If you are actively applying, time your conversations so you have at least one competing offer before you finalise anything.

Push back on the first offer, politely. In India, a first offer almost always has room. A simple response like 'I was expecting something closer to my target, is there flexibility?' opens the conversation without being confrontational. Most hiring managers expect some back-and-forth.

Negotiate joining bonus and variable separately. If the fixed component is stuck, ask about a joining bonus (especially if you are forfeiting unvested stock or a bonus cycle at your current company) or about raising the variable payout. These are often easier to move than base salary.

Get the full CTC breakup before you sign. Gratuity, retiral benefits, and certain allowances are sometimes included in CTC in ways that reduce your actual monthly in-hand amount. Always ask for a written breakup and check what lands in your account each month.

05 Total Comp Breakdown

Total Comp Breakdown

What Makes Up Your Total Compensation

CTC (Cost to Company) in India almost never equals what lands in your bank account each month. Here is what a typical QA Engineer offer package in Delhi NCR looks like:

Basic Salary: Commonly cited by HR professionals as 40-50% of total CTC. This portion drives your PF contribution, gratuity calculation, and HRA tax exemption, so a higher basic is generally better for you even if the overall CTC looks the same.

HRA (House Rent Allowance): A standard component of Indian salary structures. It is tax-exempt if you pay rent and submit valid proof. For Delhi NCR residents, this can make a real difference to your annual tax liability.

Special Allowance or Flexible Benefit Plan: Companies often park the remaining CTC here. Some firms let you allocate across meal vouchers, fuel allowance, internet reimbursement, or leave travel allowance, each carrying partial tax benefits.

Variable Pay or Performance Bonus: Publicly reported industry surveys indicate variable components at mid-level technology roles commonly sit in the 10-20% of CTC range, though actual payout depends on individual and company performance. Ask what the historical payout percentage has been at the company, not just what the policy ceiling says.

PF (Provident Fund): A fixed statutory percentage of your basic, publicly reported at 12% under current EPF rules, is contributed by both you and your employer. The employer contribution is part of your CTC but goes into your PF account, not your salary account.

Gratuity: Paid out after a minimum continuous service period (publicly reported as 5 years under the Payment of Gratuity Act). It is often included in CTC calculations but is not accessible until you complete that tenure.

ESOPs or RSUs: Less common in services companies, more common in funded startups and product firms. If you receive stock, ask about the vesting schedule, the cliff period, and the company's current valuation.

Q Questions

Frequently asked

What is the salary range for a QA Engineer in Delhi NCR in 2026?

Based on knok jobradar data from July 2026, QA Engineer salaries in Delhi NCR range from 4 LPA at entry level to 45+ LPA for lead roles. A mid-level engineer with 3-5 years of experience typically falls in the 9-17 LPA range. The exact number depends heavily on whether your work is manual or automation-focused, and on the size and type of company you are joining.

Does automation testing pay more than manual testing in Delhi NCR?

Yes, in most cases. Engineers with strong automation skills in tools like Selenium, Cypress, or Playwright consistently receive higher offers within the same experience band. Industry surveys commonly cite a meaningful salary difference between manual and automation QA professionals at the mid level. Building even one end-to-end automation framework, even outside your current job, is a strong differentiator when applying.

Is Delhi NCR a good market for QA Engineer jobs compared to Bangalore?

Both cities are strong options. Bangalore had 86 active openings in the knok jobradar data versus 65 in Delhi NCR as of July 2026, so raw volume is higher in Bangalore. Delhi NCR offers a varied mix of IT services, fintech, e-commerce, and government tech roles. Salary at Bangalore product companies is commonly cited as somewhat higher, but Delhi NCR's lower cost of living means real purchasing power can be comparable.

How do I move from manual QA to automation QA to increase my salary?

Start by picking one language (Python or JavaScript) and one framework (Pytest or Playwright) and building a small project on your own, even if your current job does not require it. Contributing to open-source test suites or maintaining a personal project gives you something concrete to show interviewers. When applying, highlight the automation work explicitly in your resume rather than burying it. Many mid-level QA roles in Delhi NCR specifically list automation as a requirement.

What skills should a QA Engineer highlight to get the best offers in 2026?

API testing (Postman, RestAssured), UI automation (Selenium, Playwright, Cypress), performance testing basics (JMeter or k6), and CI integration (Jenkins, GitHub Actions) appear most often in Delhi NCR job descriptions right now. Beyond tools, demonstrating that you understand the full software development lifecycle and can communicate clearly with developers and product managers makes a strong impression in interviews.

How do I make sure my QA application actually gets seen by HR?

Most applications go into an applicant tracking system and may sit unread for days. Applying within the first day or two of a posting, tailoring your resume to include the specific tools mentioned in the job description, and following up with the recruiter on LinkedIn all improve your chances.

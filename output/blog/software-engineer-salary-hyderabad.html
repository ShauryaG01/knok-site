01 Salary Overview

Salary Overview

Most Software Engineer job listings in India quietly skip the salary. Recruiters post a role, collect applications, and reveal the number only after a call, if at all. That is the reality behind the 183 Software Engineer openings tracked in Hyderabad as of 8 July 2026 by knok jobradar. Despite thin public data, enough offer letters, LinkedIn posts, and community threads exist to sketch a credible picture. The bands below come from knok's own job-radar data and should be read as ranges, not guarantees, company size, funding stage, and your specific skills all move the number. Hyderabad has quietly grown into a serious tech hub. Product companies, GCCs (Global Capability Centres), and startups all hire here, and compensation has tracked upward across the 2024-2026 period. If you are a Software Engineer evaluating an offer or planning your next move, these bands give you an honest floor and ceiling to negotiate from.

Entry (0-2 years): Fresh graduates and early-career engineers typically land in the ₹6-12 LPA range. Service companies tend to sit near the bottom; funded product startups and GCCs often offer closer to the top of that band for strong coders. Mid (3-5 years): This is where pay variation is widest. Engineers who have moved into a product company, led a feature end-to-end, or picked up in-demand skills like cloud or ML can reach the ₹25 LPA ceiling. Those staying in service roles often remain closer to ₹15 LPA. Senior (6-9 years): The ₹28-45 LPA band is where Hyderabad's GCCs and larger product firms compete hard. Strong distributed-systems or data-engineering experience pushes toward the upper end. Lead / Staff (10+ years): Beyond ₹40 LPA, cash comp alone is less meaningful, ESOPs and variable bonuses become a large slice. The '65+' ceiling is open-ended; publicly reported outliers at top-tier product companies exceed this.

04 Negotiation Tips

Negotiation Tips

1. Know your band before the call
Walk into every recruiter conversation knowing the range for your level from the table above. Saying 'I am expecting ₹X based on my experience and current market data' is far more effective than waiting to hear their number first. 2. Negotiate on CTC components, not just base
If a company cannot move on base, ask about the joining bonus, variable pay percentage, ESOP grant, or notice-period buyout. These are often more flexible than the headline CTC number. 3. Ask for time to decide
Asking for a day or two to review an offer is completely reasonable and professional. Any recruiter who pushes back aggressively is signalling something about the company culture. 4. Use competing offers ethically
If you have a second offer, you can mention it, but do not bluff. Recruiters in the same industry talk, and a false claim can follow you. 5. Target the 'Senior' inflection point
The jump from the top of Mid (₹25 LPA) to the Senior floor (₹28 LPA) is one of the biggest step-changes in the career ladder. If you are at four or five years, consider whether a job change is the fastest path to Senior-band pay rather than waiting for an internal increment.

05 Total Comp Breakdown

Total Comp Breakdown

Indian tech compensation is rarely 'what you see is what you get.' Here is what the CTC number typically hides: Base Salary: The fixed monthly amount. Commonly cited as 40-60% of CTC at large service companies; funded product startups and some GCCs tend toward cleaner structures with a higher base percentage. Variable Pay: Tied to individual and company performance. A target variable of 15-20% of CTC is commonly cited across mid-to-large Indian tech firms, but actual payout varies based on company performance. Joining Bonus: A one-time payment to offset the loss of unvested stock or bonus at your current employer. Often comes with a clawback clause if you leave within a year. ESOPs / RSUs: At funded startups, ESOPs can look large on paper but are illiquid until an IPO or acquisition. At listed companies or large MNCs with RSUs, vesting schedules, commonly cited as around four years, make the annual value more predictable. Allowances: HRA, LTA, medical, and phone allowances form a portion of CTC that is partially tax-exempt. Always ask HR for the full salary structure, not just the headline number. PF Contribution: Employer PF is included in CTC at many companies, reducing in-hand pay. Factor this in when comparing two offers side by side. When comparing offers, build a simple table: base + expected variable + annual ESOP value (at current valuation) + joining bonus spread across your expected tenure. That gives a more honest year-one picture.

Q Questions

Frequently asked

What is the Software Engineer salary range in Hyderabad in 2026?

Based on knok jobradar data, the range depends heavily on experience: ₹6-12 LPA for entry-level, ₹15-25 LPA for mid-level, and ₹28-45 LPA for senior engineers. 'Average' across all levels is not especially useful, knowing your specific band is far more actionable. Company type matters too: a GCC or funded product startup will typically pay more than a traditional IT services firm at the same level.

Is Hyderabad a good city for Software Engineers compared to Bangalore?

Hyderabad had 183 active Software Engineer openings in this snapshot versus Bangalore's 958, so raw volume is lower. However, Hyderabad's cost of living is commonly cited as meaningfully lower, which can make an offer in the mid-range feel more comfortable in real terms. The city's GCC ecosystem has grown strongly through 2024-2026, and many engineers prefer the slightly lower pace alongside competitive pay.

How do I know if an offer is fair when the listing has no salary range?

Use the bands in this article as your anchor: match your years of experience to the relevant row and check whether the offer sits in that range. You can also cross-check on Glassdoor or levels.fyi for specific company data points, keeping in mind that self-reported figures have sample-size limitations. If the offer is below the floor for your level, that is your starting point for a counter-offer conversation.

Do Hyderabad GCCs pay differently from product startups?

Generally, large GCCs offer structured pay, strong benefits, and predictable increments, often landing in the middle of the band for your experience level. Well-funded product startups may offer higher cash or significant ESOPs, but with more variability in both pay and job security. Industry surveys suggest GCC salaries have become more competitive through 2025-2026 as demand for tech talent increased across the city.

What skills push a Software Engineer's salary toward the top of the band?

Cloud platforms, distributed systems, and data engineering are consistently in high demand, according to industry surveys. Within Hyderabad specifically, skills aligned to GCC work, enterprise integrations, security engineering, and platform engineering, carry a premium. Demonstrating ownership (led a project end-to-end, improved a key metric) rather than just participation is what most hiring managers say they pay more for.

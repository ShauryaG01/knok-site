01 Salary Overview

Salary Overview

Frontend Engineer compensation in India varies by city, company stage, and whether the role is product, platform, or growth-focused. Fixed pay is quoted in LPA (lakhs per annum); variable pay and ESOPs are common at startups. Always clarify fixed vs CTC before negotiating.

04 Negotiation Tips

Negotiation Tips

  1. Anchor with data from 3+ offers or trusted peers at the same level.
  2. Negotiate fixed first; treat variable and ESOP separately.
  3. Ask for signing bonus if base is capped.
  4. Get the offer letter with break-up (basic, HRA, PF) before resigning.
  5. If competing offers exist, share timelines, not numbers, to accelerate decisions.
05 Total Comp Breakdown

Total Comp Breakdown

CTC typically includes: Basic, HRA, special allowance, PF employer contribution, gratuity provision, and projected variable. Ask for in-hand estimate after tax. ESOPs at startups may not appear in CTC, model dilution and vesting cliff separately.

06 Market Snapshot

Market Snapshot

As of 8 July 2026, knok's job radar found 94 active Frontend Engineer openings in Bangalore. That makes Bangalore by far the strongest market for this role, Delhi is next with 36 openings, then Pune with 9. The hiring mix here is revealing: the top 10 companies are almost entirely Indian product companies and funded startups, healthtech, fintech, AI, proptech, and Web3. No traditional GCCs (global capability centres of MNCs) appear in the top list. That tells you where current demand is coming from: companies building their own products, not offshore delivery arms of multinationals. For frontend engineers, this is good news. Product company roles tend to come with real ownership over the user experience, a faster feedback loop between your code and the end user, and, at funded startups, equity upside. The trade-off is less structured onboarding and more ambiguity than a large GCC typically offers.

At the entry level, the gap between ₹5 LPA and ₹11 LPA usually comes down to skill depth. Solid React with TypeScript and a grasp of state management moves you toward the upper end of that band. At mid and senior levels, the range reflects the difference between someone who executes tasks and someone who independently owns features and makes architecture calls. Lead and Staff roles at funded startups also commonly include ESOPs, which can significantly shift the total compensation picture even when the fixed salary looks similar to a larger company.

09 How To Stand Out

How To Stand Out

Frontend engineering has a large applicant pool in Bangalore. Here is what actually separates shortlisted candidates at the companies on this list: 1. Show product thinking, not just coding skill. These are product companies. Hiring managers want to see that you thought about why you built something, not just how. Frame your resume around impact and decisions, not just a list of technologies. 2. Go deep in React. React dominates frontend hiring across this list. Being genuinely strong, hooks, rendering behaviour, performance optimisation, state management trade-offs, matters more than knowing five frameworks at a surface level. 3. TypeScript is now expected. If your resume lists only JavaScript without TypeScript, some hiring managers will read that as falling behind the current standard. Add it if you have it; pick it up if you do not. 4. Design-system collaboration. Companies like Razorpay and Housing.com work with mature design systems. Showing Figma-to-code experience and familiarity with component libraries signals that you can work well alongside a design team. 5. AI interface experience is a differentiator right now. For Sarvam, Harvey, and TrueFoundry, having built any AI-facing UI, chat interfaces, streaming response displays, tool-call result rendering, is still relatively rare in candidate pools and catches attention fast.

10 Application Strategy

Application Strategy

Frontend roles at startups and product companies fill faster than you might expect. Here is how to approach the companies on this list: Apply early. Roles at well-known names like Razorpay attract a large number of applications within days of going live, and early applicants get more attention. Do not let a role sit in your 'to-apply' list for a week. Segment your applications. Group these companies by type, healthtech, fintech, AI, and write one tailored resume version per group. The core stays the same; the project highlights and summary shift slightly to match what each sector cares about. Message the recruiter or hiring manager directly on LinkedIn. A short, specific note, 'I noticed your Frontend Engineer opening, I have strong React experience and recently built X, happy to share more', stands out in a way that a form submission does not. Keep it one paragraph and make it specific. Prepare a portfolio or live project link. Q Questions

Frequently asked

Which company on this list is best for a fresher frontend engineer?

MediBuddy, Housing.com, and Whatfix have more structured hiring at the junior level compared to early-stage startups. These companies have defined onboarding tracks and are more likely to invest time in training a fresh graduate. Early-stage startups like Arcana or Harvey tend to prefer candidates who can contribute quickly without much hand-holding.

Do I need Web3 knowledge to apply to Arcana?

Strong React skills matter more than prior Web3 experience for a frontend role at Arcana. They primarily need engineers who can build fast, polished interfaces and are willing to learn the domain on the job. That said, if you have used a wallet SDK or built anything on a testnet, mention it, it shows genuine curiosity about the space.

What does Sarvam build, and why are they hiring frontend engineers?

Sarvam is an Indian AI startup building large language models and AI products focused on Indian languages. Their frontend hiring is likely for consumer-facing AI products, voice interfaces, and developer tools built on top of their models. If working at an Indian AI lab appeals to you, Sarvam is one of the most prominent ones actively hiring right now.

Is Noon a good target even though it is headquartered outside India?

Yes, Noon runs a significant part of its engineering from Bangalore and the role is based here. E-commerce frontend work at scale is solid experience: you will likely work on performance optimisation, conversion-focused UIs, and high-traffic product pages. Just check the job description for any time zone overlap expectations with the Middle East headquarters.

How competitive is it to get a Frontend role at Razorpay?

Razorpay is one of the most recognised fintech brands for engineering in India, so the bar is high and the competition is stiff. Expect technical interviews covering React internals, UI component design, and past product decisions you have owned. Directly messaging the recruiter on LinkedIn alongside a strong application meaningfully improves your visibility compared to applying through a form alone.

Should I apply to all 10 companies or focus on a few?

Focus on quality over volume. Pick 3 or 4 companies that match your experience level and the kind of work you want to do, then tailor your resume and outreach for each. A specific, well-prepared application to your top choices will outperform a generic one sent to all 10.

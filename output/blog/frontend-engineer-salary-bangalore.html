01 Salary Overview

Salary Overview

Frontend Engineer Salary in Bangalore (2026) The honest starting point: most job listings in Bangalore do not show a salary figure. Companies leave pay ranges off their postings so they can adjust offers based on your current CTC and how well you negotiate. That makes published data scarce, but not useless. Based on knok jobradar data covering 94 active Frontend Engineer openings in Bangalore as of 2026-07-08, here is the real picture: - Entry level (0-2 years): 5-11 LPA
- Mid level (3-5 years): 12-22 LPA
- Senior level (6-9 years): 24-40 LPA
- Lead / Staff: 38-58+ LPA These bands align with what publicly reported figures on Glassdoor and levels.fyi show for Bangalore-based frontend roles. The overlap between Senior and Lead bands is real, a strong Senior Engineer at a product company can out-earn a Lead at a services firm.

04 Negotiation Tips

Negotiation Tips

How to Negotiate Your Frontend Engineer Salary 1 Do not anchor on your current CTC
Many companies ask what you earn now and then offer a small increment on top. You are not legally required to share your current salary. Anchor on market data instead, something like: 'industry surveys and Glassdoor show mid-level Frontend Engineers in Bangalore earning 12-22 LPA, and I am targeting the upper half of that range.' 2. Get the full breakdown in writing before you resign
Variable pay, joining bonuses, and ESOPs are often mentioned verbally during the offer call. Ask for a written offer letter with each component itemised before you hand in your notice. 3. Negotiate the joining bonus separately
If the recruiter says the base is fixed, ask for a one-time joining bonus to cover unvested ESOPs or a mid-cycle bonus you are leaving behind. Companies often agree to this when they cannot move the base. 4. Use a competing offer honestly
If you have another offer, say so, do not bluff. Something like 'I have an offer at another company and would prefer to join you if you can come close on the number' opens a real conversation rather than a standoff. 5. Name your stack-specific skills explicitly
React plus TypeScript plus Next.js is the most in-demand stack in Bangalore. If you also have Core Web Vitals tuning, accessibility, or micro-frontend experience, say so in the negotiation. Specific skills justify the top of the band in a way that years of experience alone does not.

05 Total Comp Breakdown

Total Comp Breakdown

What Total Compensation Actually Includes Fixed CTC is only the base For Frontend Engineers in Bangalore, a full offer typically has several components:

ComponentWhat to Know
Base salaryThe figure on your offer letter. Paid monthly.
Performance bonusCommonly cited at 10-20% of base at product companies. Usually paid once a year.
ESOPs / RSUsIncreasingly common even at Series A startups. Vesting is typically structured over 4 years with a 1-year cliff.
WFH / internet allowanceMany companies include a monthly home-office stipend. Ask if it is not mentioned upfront.
Joining bonusOne-time payment, often used to offset unvested equity you leave behind at your current employer.

On ESOPs: the real value depends on the company's path to liquidity. Before assigning a number to your equity, ask about the current strike price, the last valuation round, and the liquidation preference structure.

Q Questions

Frequently asked

What is the salary range for a Frontend Engineer in Bangalore in 2026?

Based on knok jobradar data covering 94 active openings as of 2026-07-08, the range runs from 5-11 LPA at entry level to 38-58+ LPA for Lead and Staff roles. Mid-level engineers with 3-5 years of experience typically fall in the 12-22 LPA range. Most listings do not disclose pay, so treat these bands as a reliable guide rather than a precise figure for every company.

Does Bangalore pay more than other Indian cities for Frontend Engineers?

Generally yes, particularly at product companies and MNCs. Bangalore has 94 openings tracked by knok jobradar versus 36 in Delhi/NCR, more competition for the same talent tends to push salaries up. Industry surveys commonly cite a Bangalore premium for equivalent roles, though the exact size of that premium varies significantly by company type and seniority level.

Which frontend tech stack pays the highest salary in Bangalore?

React with TypeScript and Next.js is the most demanded combination in Bangalore job listings right now, based on knok jobradar data. Engineers who layer on performance optimisation, Core Web Vitals experience, or micro-frontend architecture are positioned at the top of their salary band. Full-stack experience extending into Node.js or GraphQL can push compensation further, especially at product startups.

How do I benchmark my salary when companies do not list pay ranges?

Start with publicly reported data on Glassdoor and levels.fyi, then cross-reference with peer conversations in engineering communities. When speaking with recruiters, ask directly, 'what is the pay band for this role?' Many will share it. Having two or three active conversations at once also gives you real market data through the actual offers you receive.

Is switching companies worth it just for a salary jump?

Industry surveys consistently show that job switches in India yield larger increases than annual internal increments, commonly cited as the most effective lever for salary growth in tech. However, factor in unvested ESOPs, any joining-bonus clawback clauses, and the ramp-up time in a new codebase before you decide. A switch makes the most financial sense when you are also moving up in seniority, not changing companies purely laterally.

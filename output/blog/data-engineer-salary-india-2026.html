01 Salary Overview

Salary Overview

Data Engineer Salary in India, 2026 Reality Check Here is the uncomfortable truth most job seekers discover too late: the majority of Data Engineer listings in India do not show the salary. You polish your resume, sit through four interview rounds, and the actual number only comes up in the final HR call, if at all. That is what makes self-reported data on platforms like Glassdoor and levels.fyi genuinely valuable, even when sample sizes are small. It is the only way to walk into a negotiation with real numbers on your side. As of July 2026, knok jobradar tracked 483 active Data Engineer openings across India's major cities. Based on listing data and publicly reported offer figures, salaries range from ₹6 LPA at the entry level to ₹65 LPA and beyond for Lead or Staff engineers. Experience is the single biggest lever, but city, company type, and your tech stack all shape where your number lands.

04 Negotiation Tips

Negotiation Tips

How to Negotiate Your Data Engineer Offer 1 Ask for the salary band upfront
Open with: 'Could you share the budgeted range for this role?' A good employer will tell you. Silence or deflection tells you something too. 2. Anchor at the senior end of your band
If your experience puts you in the mid band, target the upper half of ₹14-26 LPA rather than the midpoint. Anchoring high gives you room to settle where you actually want to land. 3. Use a real competing offer
A live competing offer is worth more than any salary guide in a negotiation. If you have one, use it. If you do not, be honest, HR will test it. 4. Negotiate ESOPs separately from base
At product companies, equity is a separate conversation from base pay. Understand the vesting schedule, the cliff period, and the company's last valuation before agreeing to trade base salary for ESOPs. 5. Get the full breakup in writing
Offer letters sometimes lump everything into 'CTC' without a breakdown. Ask for a line-by-line split, fixed base, variable, ESOPs, and joining bonus, all in the same document before you sign.

IT services vs product companies
At an IT services firm or GCC, your package is predictable, a fixed base, a modest variable, and standard benefits. At a product startup, base pay may look lower on paper but equity can be significant if the company scales. Publicly reported total-comp figures on levels.fyi show that senior engineers at well-funded Indian startups often land well above their stated base once RSUs are counted. The notice period factor
The commonly cited long notice period in Indian employment is a real switching cost. Always ask whether the new employer offers a joining bonus to bridge the income gap, or whether they will accept a shorter buyout period. Q Questions

Frequently asked

Is ₹12 LPA realistic for a fresher Data Engineer in India?

Yes, ₹12 LPA is the top of the entry band (0-2 years) and is achievable, typically at a well-funded product startup or a company with a serious data team. Freshers without specialised project experience tend to land toward the lower end of the ₹6-12 LPA range. Hands-on SQL, Python, and cloud platform exposure will shift your offer toward the upper end of that band.

Why is there such a large gap between entry and mid-level Data Engineer salaries?

The jump reflects a real shift in responsibility. At entry level you work under supervision; at mid-level (3-5 years) you are expected to own pipelines, handle production incidents, and communicate with product managers, skills that take real-world experience to build. Most engineers make this transition somewhere in the 3-5 year window, but the timing depends more on the depth of ownership you have had than the years on your resume.

Does city matter a lot for Data Engineer salaries in 2026?

Less than it used to. Many listings in Delhi NCR, Pune, and Chennai are hybrid or remote in practice, so pay differences by location are narrowing. As commonly cited in industry surveys and on Glassdoor, Bangalore and Hyderabad roles tend to carry higher base salaries, but a strong product company in any city can compete at the top of the band. Expand your search geographically before filtering by location.

What skills push a Data Engineer to the top of their salary band?

Real-time data processing (Kafka, Flink, Spark Streaming) consistently commands a premium in 2026 hiring. Strong experience with dbt for data transformation and a cloud-native warehouse, BigQuery, Redshift, or Snowflake, also keeps you competitive. Beyond technical skills, engineers who can clearly explain data quality issues to non-technical stakeholders tend to move up faster and negotiate from a stronger position.

How do I know if an offer is fair when no salary was listed?

Check Glassdoor and levels.fyi for that specific company if enough reviews exist, even small sample sizes beat guessing. Salary threads on forums like r/developersIndia are a useful secondary source, though treat them as directional rather than definitive. The strongest benchmark is a real competing offer, which turns the negotiation from guesswork into a concrete conversation.

When is the best time to negotiate, before or after the formal offer?

Negotiate after the verbal offer but before you sign. Once a company has decided they want you, they have already invested in the process and are far more flexible than at the screening stage. Asking for time to 'review the numbers and come back with a few questions' is completely standard, most good employers expect it, and agreeing on the spot rarely gets you a better deal.

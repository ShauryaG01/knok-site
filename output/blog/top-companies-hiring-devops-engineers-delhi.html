Salary Overview

DevOps Engineer compensation in India varies by city, company stage, and whether the role is product, platform, or growth-focused. Fixed pay is quoted in LPA (lakhs per annum); variable pay and ESOPs are common at startups. Always clarify fixed vs CTC before negotiating.

04 Negotiation Tips

Negotiation Tips

  1. Anchor with data from 3+ offers or trusted peers at the same level.
  2. Negotiate fixed first; treat variable and ESOP separately.
  3. Ask for signing bonus if base is capped.
  4. Get the offer letter with break-up (basic, HRA, PF) before resigning.
  5. If competing offers exist, share timelines, not numbers, to accelerate decisions.
05 Total Comp Breakdown

Total Comp Breakdown

CTC typically includes: Basic, HRA, special allowance, PF employer contribution, gratuity provision, and projected variable. Ask for in-hand estimate after tax. ESOPs at startups may not appear in CTC, model dilution and vesting cliff separately.

06 Market Snapshot

Market Snapshot

Delhi NCR had 40 active DevOps Engineer openings as of July 2026, tracked by knok jobradar. Nationally, Bangalore leads with 187 openings, but Delhi NCR ranks second among major cities, ahead of Pune (37 openings), Hyderabad (28), Chennai (13), and Mumbai (11).

The NCR market has a distinctive character. A large share of the hiring comes from GCCs (Global Capability Centres), the India engineering arms of multinational companies. These sit alongside a smaller but growing set of Indian product companies and funded startups. For DevOps professionals, this means you can choose between the stability and process maturity of a large GCC and the broader ownership you get at a startup or mid-size product company.

09 How To Stand Out

How To Stand Out

Get your cloud certifications in order. Delhi NCR's GCC-heavy market means hiring managers frequently filter by AWS, Azure, or GCP certifications. A certification does not replace hands-on experience, but it removes a common early-screening barrier, especially at Deutsche Telekom Digital Labs, Thales, and Bupa.

Specialise in at least one IaC tool. Terraform is the dominant choice across NCR job descriptions, followed by Ansible. A public GitHub repository with real Terraform modules is more convincing than a bullet point on a resume, and it gives interviewers something concrete to ask about.

Highlight security in your DevOps work. GCCs in regulated industries (financial services, healthcare, defence) increasingly look for engineers who treat security as part of the pipeline, not a separate team's problem. Experience with SAST, DAST, or secrets management tools gives you a genuine edge over candidates who list only CI/CD and Kubernetes.

Tailor your resume to the company type. For a GCC like Deutsche Telekom Digital Labs, lead with process maturity, scale, and compliance-aware infrastructure. For a startup like Huru or Travclan, lead with the breadth of what you owned and how fast you shipped. A single generic resume rarely lands well across both.

10 Application Strategy

Application Strategy

Start with the companies posting the most roles. Paytm, Hanu Software Solutions, and Deutsche Telekom Digital Labs each have 5 open DevOps roles right now. Applying to all open roles at one company before moving on maximises the chance that your resume lands in front of the same recruiter more than once, which increases recall at shortlisting time.

Do not skip the smaller employers. Wingify, Wishlink, and Travclan each have 1 open role. These receive far fewer applications than the larger names, so your chances are proportionally better if you match the tech stack.

Apply early in the posting cycle. DevOps roles at GCCs in NCR move quickly at the screening stage. Applying in the first week of a posting puts you in the initial shortlist. Applying later means competing with candidates already in the interview pipeline.

Network inside the company before your interview. LinkedIn introductions to DevOps engineers or engineering managers at your target company are common in NCR, partly because the GCC community is well-connected. A warm intro does not guarantee a hire, but it can speed up the recruiter response considerably.

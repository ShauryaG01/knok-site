01 Salary Overview

Salary Overview

Data Scientist Salary in Bangalore (2026) Here is something most job seekers discover the hard way: the majority of Data Scientist job listings in India do not show the pay upfront. Recruiters often leave the salary field blank or write 'as per industry standards', which tells you almost nothing useful. To cut through that noise, knok's jobradar tracked 189 active Data Scientist openings in Bangalore as of 8 July 2026. By cross-referencing offer letters, public disclosures, and community data, we can give you real salary ranges to walk into your next interview with. Data Scientist salaries in Bangalore range from ₹8 LPA for freshers to ₹70+ LPA at the Lead and Principal level. The wide spread reflects just how different the role can be, a 'Data Scientist' at a startup building its first ML pipeline and a Principal Data Scientist at a product unicorn are doing very different work. Bangalore remains India's top market for this role, with 189 openings, more than Delhi (55), Hyderabad (38), Mumbai (22), Pune (19), and Chennai (10) combined.

04 Negotiation Tips

Negotiation Tips

How to Negotiate Your Data Scientist Salary 1 Anchor to the market band, not your last CTC
Many Indian companies ask for your previous salary and nudge you up by a small increment. Push back clearly: 'I am targeting a range in line with the market for this experience level and skill set.' Use the bands in this article as your reference point. 2. Translate model work into business outcomes
Recruiters respond to business impact, not technical jargon. Instead of saying 'I built a churn model,' say 'I built a churn model the product team used to reduce cancellations.' Specific impact equals a stronger negotiating position. 3. Ask about variable pay and equity before the offer stage
Base salary is only one component. Stocks, annual bonuses, and joining bonuses can meaningfully change what you actually take home. Ask early: 'Can you walk me through the full compensation structure?' 4. Compare offers across company types carefully
A ₹30 LPA offer at a service company and a ₹30 LPA offer at a funded startup with ESOPs are not the same package. Factor in equity, growth trajectory, and skill development when deciding. 5. Do not accept or reject on the spot
It is completely professional to say: 'Thank you, I would like 48 hours to look this over.' Use that time to check levels.fyi or Glassdoor for comparable roles and come back with a specific counter if the number is below market. 6. Specialise to command a premium
Generalist data scientists face more competition. Skills in LLM fine-tuning, MLOps, and real-time ML systems are publicly reported as commanding a significant premium in 2025-2026 hiring cycles.

05 Total Comp Breakdown

Total Comp Breakdown

What Makes Up a Data Scientist's Total Compensation in Bangalore The salary bands above refer to CTC (Cost to Company), which is how most Indian offers are quoted. CTC is not what you take home. Here is how it typically breaks down: Fixed Base Salary
The largest component of your CTC, the amount credited to your account every month before tax. This is the number to focus on first in any negotiation. Variable / Performance Bonus
Typically paid annually or semi-annually, tied to individual and company performance. At product companies this is usually a real, reliable number. At many service companies, hitting the full variable component is not guaranteed. Stock Options / ESOPs / RSUs
This is where senior compensation gets interesting. At funded startups and large tech companies, equity can form a substantial portion of the total package. It vests over time, and industry surveys consistently show that Data Scientists who negotiate equity early end up with meaningfully higher long-term compensation than those who focus only on base. Joining Bonus
Some companies offer a one-time joining bonus, particularly if they are asking you to forgo unvested equity at your current employer. This is a legitimate ask and worth raising. Perks and Benefits
Health insurance, food allowance, learning budget, remote-work allowance, real value, but often overstated in CTC calculations. Focus your negotiation energy on fixed base and equity first. The practical rule: When comparing two offers, look at fixed base plus realistic variable plus equity value, not just the headline CTC figure.

Q Questions

Frequently asked

Why do most Data Scientist job listings in India not show the salary?

Most Indian employers, particularly service companies and GCCs, prefer to keep salary ranges internal so they can make offers based on a candidate's current CTC rather than the market rate. This puts job seekers at an information disadvantage. The best workaround is to use data from Glassdoor, levels.fyi, and community forums, and to ask the recruiter for a band early in the conversation before investing time in multiple interview rounds.

What is the salary range for a Data Scientist in Bangalore with 3-5 years of experience?

Based on knok jobradar data (189 active Bangalore listings as of 8 July 2026), the mid-level band for 3-5 years of experience is ₹18-30 LPA. Where you land within that range depends heavily on company type, product versus service, and whether you have hands-on experience deploying models to production rather than only working in a notebook or analytics environment.

How does a Data Scientist salary in Bangalore compare to Hyderabad?

Bangalore has 189 active Data Scientist openings versus 38 in Hyderabad (knok jobradar, 8 July 2026). Hyderabad's market is heavily GCC-driven, with large tech campuses offering stable and structured compensation. Senior Data Scientists chasing the upper end of the ₹45-70+ LPA band tend to find more opportunities in Bangalore's product and AI-first startup ecosystem, where equity can also add meaningfully to total pay.

What skills push a Data Scientist to the higher end of the salary band?

Skills in MLOps, LLM fine-tuning, real-time feature engineering, and production ML systems are publicly reported as commanding a premium in 2025-2026 hiring. On the softer side, Data Scientists who can translate model outputs into clear business decisions, not just technical recommendations, are consistently rated higher by hiring managers and tend to negotiate more effectively than those who focus only on modelling skills.

Is total compensation for Lead and Principal Data Scientists higher than the stated salary bands?

Yes, significantly so at product companies and well-funded startups. The ₹45-70+ LPA Lead and Principal band reflects base salary; total compensation including RSUs, ESOPs, and annual bonuses is commonly cited as going well beyond that figure at top-tier product companies. Always ask for the full equity breakdown, grant size, vesting schedule, and current fair market value, when you receive an offer at this level.

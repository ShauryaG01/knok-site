01 Hiring Overview

Hiring Overview

Agoda is a travel technology company under the Booking Holdings group, running one of the most widely used hotel and flight booking platforms in Asia-Pacific. Its teams handle large transaction volumes, which creates a steady need for engineers, data scientists, product managers, analysts, and operations professionals.

As of 2026-07-08, Agoda has 299 open roles listed across departments, covering everything from backend engineering and machine learning to finance, customer support, and business development. That is a healthy pipeline and a good window to apply.

Agoda's India hiring is concentrated in tech and data roles, though the company also recruits for regional business functions. The hiring process is thorough and data-driven, which reflects the company's internal culture. Expect multiple rounds and be ready to back your answers with clear reasoning at every stage.

03 Interview Process

Interview Process

Agoda's hiring process typically runs across five to six stages, though the exact number varies by role and team. Tech roles tend to have more rounds than business or operations roles.

Stage 1: Application and resume screen. Your resume goes through an internal recruiter review. Agoda looks for relevant experience, a record of measurable impact, and skills that map to the job description. Tailoring your resume to each specific role helps you clear this stage.

Stage 2: Recruiter call. A short introductory call with a talent acquisition person. They check your background, notice period, and compensation expectations. Use this time to ask how many rounds the process has and what each stage covers.

Stage 3: Online assessment or take-home task. For engineering roles, candidates commonly report a coding test covering data structures and algorithms. Data roles may include an analytical case or an SQL-heavy assignment. Product roles sometimes receive a written case study. This stage filters for fundamentals before live interviews begin.

Stage 4: Technical interviews. This is typically the most involved part of the process. Engineering candidates usually go through two to three technical rounds covering algorithms, system design, and behavioural questions woven in. Data roles often include a statistics or case-based round. Interviewers probe reasoning, not just final answers.

Stage 5: Hiring manager or cross-functional round. A conversation focused on your past projects, how you handle ambiguity, and how you would contribute to the team. This round carries significant weight. Be ready to walk through specific work in detail, including what went wrong and how you corrected course.

Stage 6: Offer and reference check. If the loop goes well, you receive a verbal or written offer. Reference checks may happen before or after the offer depending on the team and function.

04 What They Look For

What They Look For

Agoda consistently signals a few qualities across its job descriptions and in feedback shared publicly by candidates who have been through the process.

Strong fundamentals. For engineering and data roles, core skills matter more than a long list of tools. Interviewers are known to test fundamentals rigorously, whether that is algorithms for engineers or probability and statistics for data scientists.

Ownership mindset. The company prefers people who take full accountability for outcomes, not just tasks. Interviewers often ask for examples of driving something end-to-end, including how you handled obstacles along the way.

Data-backed thinking. Even in non-technical roles, Agoda expects you to reason with data. Product and business candidates who bring metrics into their answers consistently do better than those who give qualitative-only responses.

Cross-cultural communication. Agoda teams are international by design. The ability to work across cultures, time zones, and functions comes up repeatedly in feedback from candidates at multiple levels.

Speed paired with quality. The travel industry moves fast. Agoda wants people who can ship, iterate, and learn quickly, while still caring about the end customer experience.

05 How To Prepare

How To Prepare

Map your resume to the job description. Agoda's job descriptions are often detailed. Before applying, match your experience point by point to the listed requirements and rewrite bullet points to reflect that alignment clearly.

Practise coding fundamentals if you are in a tech role. Candidates commonly report questions on arrays, trees, graphs, dynamic programming, and system design for senior positions. Time yourself on practice problems and do not just read solutions passively.

Build a STAR story bank. Agoda behavioural questions focus on ownership, handling failure, and cross-team collaboration. Prepare four to five strong examples you can adapt to different questions. Each story should include a measurable outcome.

Read up on the travel tech space. Understanding how Agoda makes money, who its competitors are, and what challenges online travel agencies face in Asia will help you in hiring manager rounds and product interviews. Go in with a point of view.

Know the numbers behind your past work. If you are in product, analytics, or business, be ready to talk through the metrics behind your projects. Answers that describe impact without supporting data will cost you in the evaluation.

Ask thoughtful questions in every round. Agoda interviewers note that candidates who ask specific questions about the team, product direction, or technical challenges leave a stronger impression than those who say they have no questions.

06 Culture And Values

Culture And Values

Agoda runs a culture that is openly data-driven and performance-oriented. The company expects people to move fast, take initiative, and make decisions backed by evidence rather than intuition alone.

International by default. Agoda's workforce spans dozens of nationalities and collaboration across locations is built into how teams operate. New joiners from India often mention that the environment feels genuinely flat, where ideas are evaluated on merit regardless of seniority.

High ownership culture. Teams are small relative to the problems they solve. That means individuals carry real responsibility early. People who thrive here tend to be comfortable with ambiguity and proactive about identifying what needs to be done without waiting to be told.

Direct feedback. The culture values honesty over politeness. Feedback is given candidly and expected to be received constructively. If you are used to indirect or hierarchical feedback styles, this takes some adjustment.

Continuous learning and internal mobility. Agoda invests in skill development and actively encourages people to move across teams over time. Many employees publicly report that this makes it a strong place to broaden your profile beyond a single specialisation.

For Indian professionals coming from large IT services companies, the culture shift can feel significant. Agoda is more product-focused, faster-paced, and less process-heavy than traditional enterprise environments. That is a feature for some and an adjustment for others.

Q Questions

Frequently asked

How many interview rounds does Agoda typically have?

Agoda's process typically runs five to six stages from application to offer. Tech roles tend to have more rounds than business roles, often including an online assessment plus two to three live interview rounds. The exact number varies by team, so it is worth asking the recruiter upfront how many stages to expect and what each one covers.

Does Agoda hire freshers or only experienced candidates?

Agoda does hire fresh graduates, particularly for engineering roles through campus and entry-level programs. Most of the 299 open roles listed as of 2026-07-08 are for professionals with some years of experience, however. If you are a fresher, filter specifically for 'graduate' or 'entry-level' tags in the job listings to find the right openings.

How long does the Agoda hiring process take from application to offer?

Based on publicly reported candidate feedback, the process can take anywhere from a few weeks to a couple of months, depending on the role and how quickly rounds are scheduled. Tech roles with multiple rounds tend to take longer than business or operations roles. Following up politely with your recruiter after each stage can help keep your loop moving.

Can I apply to more than one Agoda role at the same time?

Yes, applying to multiple roles is generally acceptable as long as each role genuinely fits your background. Agoda recruiters coordinate internally, so if you are in a loop for one role they may flag you for another that is a better match. Be upfront with your recruiter about the other roles you have applied to so they can help route you correctly.

What kind of coding questions should I expect in the Agoda technical round?

Candidates commonly report questions on data structures, algorithms, and system design in Agoda's engineering interviews. Fundamental topics like trees, graphs, arrays, and dynamic programming come up frequently for software engineering roles. For senior positions, system design questions covering scalability and distributed systems are a regular part of the loop.

Is Agoda a good company for Indian professionals looking for international exposure?

Agoda has a strong reputation for international exposure given its multi-national teams and Asia-Pacific focus. Indian professionals who have worked there publicly report learning to collaborate across cultures and taking on broader ownership earlier than in traditional Indian IT setups. Whether it leads to physical relocation depends on the role and your goals, but cross-cultural experience is built into the day-to-day work from day one.

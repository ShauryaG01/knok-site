01 Salary Overview

Salary Overview

Product Designer compensation in India varies by city, company stage, and whether the role is product, platform, or growth-focused. Fixed pay is quoted in LPA (lakhs per annum); variable pay and ESOPs are common at startups. Always clarify fixed vs CTC before negotiating.

04 Negotiation Tips

Negotiation Tips

  1. Anchor with data from 3+ offers or trusted peers at the same level.
  2. Negotiate fixed first; treat variable and ESOP separately.
  3. Ask for signing bonus if base is capped.
  4. Get the offer letter with break-up (basic, HRA, PF) before resigning.
  5. If competing offers exist, share timelines, not numbers, to accelerate decisions.
05 Total Comp Breakdown

Total Comp Breakdown

CTC typically includes: Basic, HRA, special allowance, PF employer contribution, gratuity provision, and projected variable. Ask for in-hand estimate after tax. ESOPs at startups may not appear in CTC, model dilution and vesting cliff separately.

Q Questions

Frequently asked

What is the average Product Designer salary in India in 2026?

For a Product Designer in India in 2026, pay scales with experience: entry (0-2 yrs) around 6-11 LPA, mid (3-5 yrs) around 14-22 LPA, senior (6-9 yrs) around 24-38 LPA, and lead/staff (10+ yrs) around 35-50+ LPA. These are fixed figures in LPA (lakhs per annum) and vary by city and company stage, Bangalore and Hyderabad usually top the range for tech IC roles. Startups add ESOPs on top, while GCCs lean towards a higher fixed base with less equity.

Should I negotiate fixed pay or variable first?

Always lock the fixed base first, because that's the number that compounds into future raises and your next offer. Treat variable pay and ESOPs as separate line items with their own vesting and payout conditions, and don't let a large 'total CTC' headline distract you from a modest fixed component. If the base is capped by a band, ask for a signing bonus to bridge the gap.

Is CTC the same as my in-hand salary?

No, and the gap is often larger than people expect. CTC bundles your fixed pay with the employer's PF contribution, gratuity provisions, and a projected (not guaranteed) variable component. Ask explicitly for an in-hand monthly estimate after tax and deductions, and request the offer letter with the full break-up, basic, HRA, PF, and allowances, before you resign anywhere.

How much do ESOPs actually add to my compensation?

At startups, equity can meaningfully boost total compensation, but it's the riskiest part of the package. ESOPs usually vest over four years with a one-year cliff, and their value depends on the company's exit and any future dilution. Model them separately from cash, understand the strike price and vesting schedule, and never accept a below-market base purely on the promise of equity.

When should I share my current or expected salary?

Hold off until you understand the role's scope and level, then share a researched range rather than a precise current number. Anchor the range to peers at your target level in the same city, using two or three data points from trusted contacts or offers. Sharing a range keeps you from anchoring the negotiation too low while signalling you've done your homework.

01 Salary Overview

Salary Overview

Most Software Engineer listings in India hide the salary. You clear two or three rounds and only learn the number at the end, which may not match what you expected. That is the ground reality heading into 2026. Based on knok jobradar data as of 8 July 2026, there are 171 active Software Engineer openings in Delhi NCR. Salaries run from 6 LPA for fresh graduates to 65+ LPA for lead and staff engineers with a decade of experience. The label 'Software Engineer' covers roles from product startups at the top of the band to IT services firms at the lower end, so two listings with the same title can represent very different offers. Before you benchmark your offer, do two things: get a line-by-line CTC breakup (not just the headline number), and confirm whether the company is a product firm, a funded startup, or an IT services company. Those three categories occupy different parts of the same band, and comparing across them without that context will give you a skewed picture.

04 Negotiation Tips

Negotiation Tips

1. Get the CTC breakup before comparing anything
Ask for a line-by-line breakdown: fixed base, variable target, joining bonus, gratuity, PF, and ESOPs. Two offers at the same headline number can mean very different monthly take-home once you separate fixed cash from contingent pay. 2. Anchor your ask with published data
When you counter an offer, reference publicly reported ranges on Glassdoor or levels.fyi for Software Engineer roles in Delhi NCR. 'Glassdoor shows this role at X-Y LPA in this city' lands better in negotiation than 'I was expecting more.' 3. Switch to move up the band, not just to leave
Industry surveys consistently show that changing employers is the fastest route from the bottom of your experience band to the top. Internal hikes at most Indian companies rarely keep pace with market movement. 4. At senior levels, push for equity and variable too
A better ESOP grant or a higher variable target can add more long-term value than a base hike, especially at funded startups where equity has real upside. Do not leave that conversation on the table. 5. Give one number, not a range
Say 'I am targeting X LPA fixed' rather than 'somewhere between X and Y.' A single number signals that you know your worth; a range invites the recruiter to anchor at the lower end.

05 Total Comp Breakdown

Total Comp Breakdown

The CTC on an offer letter is rarely what lands in your bank account each month. Here is what a typical Software Engineer package in Delhi NCR actually contains: - Fixed base salary, the monthly amount credited to your account before TDS and PF deductions
- Variable / performance bonus, paid annually; Glassdoor reviews for Delhi NCR tech employers frequently flag a gap between the stated target and what engineers actually receive
- Joining bonus, one-time payment, typically clawed back if you leave within a year
- Gratuity and employer PF, included in your CTC figure but only accessible when you leave or retire; do not count this as spendable income today
- ESOPs / RSUs, standard at funded startups and larger tech firms, with vesting schedules that typically run 4 years and a 1-year cliff before anything vests When comparing two offers, the number that matters most is fixed salary plus last year's actual variable payout, not the target. For startup offers, ask when the last valuation was done before putting weight on the ESOP figure. Q Questions

Frequently asked

Is the 15-25 LPA mid-level band realistic for a Software Engineer in Delhi NCR with 3-5 years of experience?

Yes, knok jobradar data as of July 2026 shows 15-25 LPA as the standard mid-level range in Delhi NCR. Product companies tend to pay toward the upper half of the band; IT services roles often sit closer to the lower end. Switching employers is typically the fastest way to move from the bottom of that band toward the middle, since internal appraisal cycles rarely close that gap quickly.

Do Delhi NCR Software Engineer salaries match what engineers earn in Bangalore?

Not typically, based on publicly reported data on Glassdoor and levels.fyi. Bangalore commands a clear premium, driven by the concentration of product companies and global tech offices, 958 active openings versus Delhi NCR's 171 reflects that gap in market depth. Industry surveys show Hyderabad is narrowing the difference at senior levels, while Delhi NCR's mix of IT services and product roles keeps the city average below pure product-company markets.

A job listing does not show any salary, how do I find out what the role actually pays?

Check Glassdoor and levels.fyi for the company and role before your first recruiter call, and come in with a specific target number rather than a vague range. When the recruiter asks what you are expecting, name a figure anchored to that research, saying 'open to discussion' hands them control of the anchor. This is especially important in Delhi NCR, where the same 'Software Engineer' title can span a very wide range depending on employer type.

What should I know about variable pay before accepting a Software Engineer offer in Delhi NCR?

Variable pay is commonly cited as a small percentage of CTC at IT services firms, and more meaningful at product companies and funded startups. The key question to ask during the offer stage is what percentage of employees actually received their full variable payout last year, not what the stated target is. Industry surveys show the actual payout frequently falls short of the headline target, particularly in years with tough performance thresholds.

How often should I switch jobs to stay competitive on salary as a Software Engineer?

Industry surveys consistently show that changing employers every few years is the most reliable way to reach the top of your experience band. Annual internal hikes at most Indian companies are commonly cited as below market movement for skilled engineers, which means staying put often means falling behind in real terms. Aim for at least 2 years at each company so that a pattern of short tenures does not raise flags with recruiters at your next move.

What hidden deductions should I look for in a Delhi NCR offer letter?

The biggest gap between headline CTC and actual take-home comes from three places: variable pay counted at target but paid at actuals, retiral contributions like gratuity and employer PF that are included in the CTC figure but locked away until you leave, and joining bonuses with clawback clauses that make them conditional for at least a year. At startups, the ESOP valuation on your offer letter may reflect an older funding round rather than current market value. Always ask for a full breakup and confirm the fixed cash component before you sign.

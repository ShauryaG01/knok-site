01 Market Snapshot

Market Snapshot

As of July 2026, knok's jobradar tracks 917 open software engineer roles in Bangalore across the boards it monitors. The headline isn't the number, it's the mix. Three distinct segments are hiring at once: global capability centres (JPMorgan Chase, Visa, Cisco, Oracle, HPE, The Walt Disney Company) running large India engineering hubs; global product companies (Databricks, Okta) building core product from Bangalore; and Indian startups (KrazyBee, Pocket FM, Sarvam AI) hiring for speed and breadth. Each segment interviews differently, pays differently, and responds to different application strategies, treating them as one undifferentiated "Bangalore market" is the most common mistake candidates make. 04 Salary Range

Salary Range

None of the top employers in this snapshot disclose salary in their listings, that's the honest picture, and it's typical of the Bangalore market, where pay is negotiated at offer stage rather than advertised. For directional context only: commonly cited market ranges put early-career engineers at large GCCs and services-adjacent firms roughly at ₹10-25 LPA, global product companies (the Databricks and Okta tier) frequently cited at ₹30-60+ LPA for mid-level engineers including stock, and funded Indian startups somewhere between, often trading cash for ESOPs. These are hedged market impressions, not knok data, the only reliable numbers are the ones you negotiate, so anchor on the segment's citation range and your competing offers rather than a listing's silence.

05 How To Stand Out

How To Stand Out

Match your signal to the segment. GCCs (JPMorgan Chase, Visa, Cisco, Oracle, HPE, Disney) screen for solid CS fundamentals, DSA performance, and stability, certifications and domain exposure (payments at Visa, banking at JPMorgan) genuinely help, and referrals move applications out of high-volume ATS queues. Global product companies (Databricks, Okta) hold the highest technical bar in this list: expect rigorous DSA plus distributed-systems design, and stand out with open-source contributions, systems depth, or infra-adjacent work that mirrors their product. Startups (KrazyBee, Pocket FM, Sarvam AI, Hyreo, Arcana) weight shipped-things evidence over pedigree, a deployed side project, a fast take-home, and demonstrated breadth beat a long tenure at a big brand. Across all three: a two-line outreach note to the hiring manager referencing something specific about their stack typically outperforms one more cold application. 06 Application Strategy

Application Strategy

Run three parallel tracks rather than one spray-and-pray list. For GCCs, apply through careers portals early in the posting's life and simultaneously hunt a referral on LinkedIn, employee referrals are the single highest-leverage move at high-application-volume companies. For product companies, invest before applying: two to three weeks of DSA and system design prep per company, because their loops are unforgiving and a failed attempt typically triggers a cooldown period. For startups, speed wins, apply within days of a posting going live, email the founder or engineering lead directly, and lead with something you've built. Sequence deliberately: interview at your lower-stakes options first, and stagger applications so offers land within the same few weeks for negotiating leverage. Q Questions

Frequently asked

Which companies are hiring the most software engineers in Bangalore right now?

In knok's July 2026 jobradar snapshot, Okta leads with 35 tracked openings, followed by JPMorgan Chase (26), Databricks (25), The Walt Disney Company (16), and HPE and Hyreo (14 each). Counts change daily, so treat the table as a snapshot, not a standings board.

Why don't these listings show salaries?

None of the top employers in this snapshot disclose pay in listings, the Bangalore norm is negotiating at offer stage. Use commonly cited market ranges for your segment as a directional anchor, and rely on competing offers rather than listed figures for leverage.

Is it better to join a GCC, a global product company, or a startup in Bangalore?

It depends on what you're optimising for. GCCs typically offer stability and structured growth; global product companies like Databricks and Okta are commonly associated with the highest compensation and technical bar; startups such as Sarvam AI or Pocket FM offer breadth, speed, and equity upside with more risk. Many engineers rotate across segments over a career.

How competitive is the Bangalore software engineer market in 2026?

Openings are healthy, 917 tracked software engineer roles in this snapshot alone, but application volumes at name-brand companies are high, so differentiation matters more than volume. Referrals, targeted outreach, and segment-appropriate preparation typically beat mass applications.

Do these companies hire remote, or is Bangalore presence required?

Policies vary by company and team, and most listings in this snapshot are posted against Bangalore as the base location. GCCs typically expect hybrid office presence, while some startups and global product companies are more flexible, confirm the policy in the specific listing or with the recruiter before investing in the process.

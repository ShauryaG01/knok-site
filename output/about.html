01 Our mission

Our mission

knok helps ambitious professionals in India find, apply to, and track high-fit roles without spending 15+ hours a week on repetitive job-board work. We combine automated board scanning, tailored applications, and open career research.

02 What we publish

What we publish

Career guides on this site are produced by our research team using:

  • A live index of 100,000+ job postings across India and remote-friendly roles
  • Disclosed salary data from indexed listings (where employers publish CTC)
  • Interview frameworks validated against hiring loops at Indian startups and MNCs

Pages are updated when our job index refreshes — typically daily.

knok tracks these jobs live, checks each one against your resume, and even writes the message to HR for you.
